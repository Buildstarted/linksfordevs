Ditto PR – strategy. execution. impact.

strategy. execution. impact.

From results to client relationships to communications counsel, Ditto is a “better PR firm.” We believe in giving individualized attention to each account by a full team of senior level staff who are passionate about our clients’ issues and have prior experience in the clients’ industries.
Our clients are always first. Our mantra is no up-selling, no over-billing, no buzz words. We learn about your company, develop a smart, manageable strategy and execute it immediately. As a result of this approach, some call Ditto “the anti-PR agency.”

Media Relations

We generate media coverage that puts our clients in front of their target audience and positively impacts their business. We work with local, trade and national media on a daily basis. Ditto specializes in getting clients on-going, month-to-month media coverage and positioning them as leaders of industry.

Message + Media Training

Whether you are launching a product or service or looking to drive awareness, messaging makes the difference. We refine POV, craft audience specific talking points, and get clients ready for live interviews. As a result, clients deliver a message that will generate media coverage.

Crisis Communications & Tech Policy Relations

Something can go wrong in an instant, and you need to prepare for the worst. Whether an organization needs a preemptive crisis plan or guidance during a hack, natural disaster or any other compromising situation, our team is ready to jump in at any point to assess each situation and develop the appropriate strategy to guide a company’s response.

Events + Conferences

Ditto’s clients are speaking and participating at the world’s top conferences in their industries. We know your time is valuable. Ditto identifies conference and speaking engagements that will best reach all your target audiences and impact your business.

Op-Eds + Bylines

As a result of our knowledge of clients’ industries and our relationships with the media, Ditto has established a streamlined process and a reliable list of publications that we regularly place contributed content in. We regularly work with Fast Company, Bloomberg and CNBC.com, among others.

Social Strategy

One aspect of having a comprehensive communications campaign is digital strategy. From Facebook and Twitter to LinkedIn and influencer relations, social platforms should be utilized to build brand recognition, create market differentiation and establish POV. Ditto specializes in content creation and socialized thought leadership.
