Become a Member | Mathematical Association of America

Reading time: 3-4 minutes
Posted here: 27 Feb 2020

https://www.maa.org/join

Departmental Memberships run from September 1 - August 31 of each year.
Check out our new pricing option for 2-year Colleges!

If you would like to apply for Departmental Membership outside of the open enrollment period, please call Member Services at 800-331-1622.

You will be able to choose between the one-time or monthly payment options on the checkout page

As an MAA Member, you will receive electronic subscriptions to all MAA journals and magazines (The American Mathematical Monthly, The College Mathematics Journal, Mathematics Magazine, Math Horizons, and MAA FOCUS) for only $175 per year! Your membership also includes a print subscription to MAA FOCUS, membership in your local section, deep discounts on JMM, MathFest, and MAA publications, and more.

MAA Member Plus Print members receive all the benefits of an MAA Member, plus membership in up to 3 SIGMAAs, and print subscriptions to all MAA journals for only $249 per year!

Life Members receive all the benefits of a regular MAA membership without the need to renew each year. 

NEW!  In recognition of the evolving landscape of faculty positions, we now offer VITAL membership. The acronym “VITAL” stands for Visitors, Instructors, TAs, Adjuncts, and Lecturers, however, we know that there is considerable variation in the naming of these positions. This category of membership is intended for instructors employed at MAA Departmental Member institutions who are not included in the usual tenure and/or promotion process and whose compensation is not commensurate with faculty who are in those positions. Depending on the institution, this may variously include part-time instructors, non-tenure track instructors, and employees in other ancillary instructional positions. (To qualify, you must be at an institution with a current MAA Departmental Membership and provide a validation code, which you can get from your Departmental Membership Administrator or Department Chair.)

Included with your $35 annual dues investment are electronic subscriptions to all MAA journals and magazines (The American Mathematical Monthly, The College Mathematics Journal, Mathematics Magazine, Math Horizons, and MAA FOCUS), membership in your local section, deep discounts on JMM, MathFest, and MAA publications, and more.
Note: Please provide proof of your current full-time enrollment. Please submit one of the following items for verification:

  • A letter from the registrar's office confirming current full-time enrollment in a degree-granting program.
  • A class schedule confirming current full-time enrollment in a degree-granting program.
