With on-demand event replay, you have the ability to take a completely shut-down system to up and running in an instant.

Use our CLI tool plumber to interact with Batch and your message queues.

plumber is an open-source CLI tool that you can use for inspecting, debugging, piping and redirecting data streams in your message queues. We use it ourselves for working with our own message queues and you should too.

Use our dashboard to view collection activity stats, analyze events and kick off replays.

Our dashboard provides you with a way to gain insight into your message queues. Whether it's one thousand messages or five billion messages, our dashboard will enable you to sift through your data with ease and let you pinpoint the exact data that you want to replay.
