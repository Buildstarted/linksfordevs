elmah.io is error logging and uptime monitoring for ASP.NET and Core

by Scott Hanselman Principal Program Manager at Microsoft
Reading time: 1 minute
Posted here: 21 Apr 2020

https://elmah.io/

Senior .NET Developer at P&RO Solutions - Bringing efficiency and effectivenes to plant management teams

elmah.io is amazingly easy to implement. You put in a few lines of code and it just works.

We were looking for a good way to be notified when any of our systems were down. By combining .NET Core Health Checks with elmah.io, we are immediately notified if any of our Windows services are not running, if configured database queries fail, or any configured endpoints are not responding. elmah.io has provided a way for us to be aware of problems before our customer.
