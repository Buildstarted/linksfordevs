Looking for a pixel artist

I’m currently working on a short RPG under the extremely working title of She said “get in the car, loser, I know you’re not up to anything this summer, I’ve got the Sword of Fate, and there’s an ancient evil that needs to be sealed away,” and I’m looking to hire a pixel artist to help realize it. It’s a modern fantasy RPG set during a road trip, following four queer losers as they fight across a countryside infested by the ancient Machine Devil.

The artist will be creating assets working closely with an existing concept artist (Isaac Robin, creator of Witch Trade!) for everything, including character animations, monsters, environments, and dialogue portraits. The art style will need to be similar to SNES RPGs—think along the lines of Chrono Trigger. Since this is my first time branching out from visual novels, we’re specifically looking for someone with a lot of experience that can help us make decisions about scope.

This will be a paid job, and we’re looking for someone with availability starting in December. If you’re interested, please send an email containing a link to your portfolio and previous game experience to christine@loveconquersallgames.com with the subject line “GET IN THE CAR PIXEL ARTIST.”

Any RTs or reblogs would be appreciated. Thank you!

The position has now been filled. Thanks to the over 60 people who submitted!
