Announcing DirectX Developer Day | DirectX Developer Blog

by Claire AndrewsFollow
Reading time: 2 minutes
Posted here: 23 Mar 2020

https://devblogs.microsoft.com/directx/announcing-directx-developer-day/

March 17th, 2020

GDC may have been postponed, but we aren’t going to let that prevent us from bringing the next generation of graphics innovation to the world.  Join us this Thursday, March 19, for a virtual DirectX Developer Day on Mixer! The Microsoft DirectX team, along with partners from AMD and NVIDIA, will be streaming a series of talks covering how we’re pushing gaming graphics into the future, introducing exciting new features, showing never-before-seen demos, and doing a deep dive into both PIX and our shader compiler.

Mark your calendar to tune in Thursday for DirectX Developer Day at 10:00 AM PDT, on https://mixer.com/DirectX. The graphics engineering team will be available online to answer your questions in real-time during our first ever Mixer stream!

Until then, join our Microsoft Game Stack colleagues for Game Stack Live today and tomorrow on https://mixer.com/gamestacklive, to learn about the latest cloud and game development technologies, go behind the scenes with the creators at Xbox Game Studios and the ID@Xbox program, and level up with technical talks and panel discussions led by industry leaders.

See you on Mixer!
