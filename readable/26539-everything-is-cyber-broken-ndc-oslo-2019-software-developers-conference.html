From EV to HPKP to certificate revocation, everything is broken. Security constructs that were once held dear are increasingly shown to be ineffective, detrimental or downright dangerous. Yet so many within the industry persist with pushing the constructs of yesteryear or forging ahead with new ones they don’t yet fully understand.

In this talk, Scott Helme and Troy Hunt will discuss the flaws within so many of these implementations. They’ll dive into what makes them ineffective and we’re we keep going wrong with them. It’s a very pragmatic, real world look into the practical value of many of these technologies. Many of them are fantastic but misunderstood, whilst many others are outright cyber-broken.
