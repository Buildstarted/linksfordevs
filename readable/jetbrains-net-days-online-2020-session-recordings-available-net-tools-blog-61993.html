JetBrains .NET Days Online 2020 – Session recordings available - .NET Tools Blog

by Posted on
Reading time: 2-3 minutes
Posted here: 22 May 2020

https://blog.jetbrains.com/dotnet/2020/05/21/jetbrains-net-days-online-2020-session-recordings-available/

Hooray! The talks from JetBrains .NET Days Online 2020 are now available for everyone to watch.

Thanks again to everyone who joined us live on May 13 and 14! Around 3200 people attended the sessions and asked more than 600 questions. If you missed any of the talks, now you can enjoy them all at your leisure. Feel free to share any of the videos in this blog post, or share the link to the event page.

Refactoring to Patterns with ReSharper, by Zoran Horvat

Yield Return ’cause I’m Awaiting: Deep Dive into Async Streams, by Andrew Karpov

From C# to Python – 10 Things I Learned Along the Way, by Tess Ferrandez

Formatting F# Code, by Florian Verdonck

Starting a Blog, by Khalid Abuhakmeh

Build a Highly Performant Interservice Communication with gRPC for ASP.NET Core, by Riccardo Terrell

Learning F# by Designing Your Own Language, by Alexey Golub

Create Web APIs with ASP.NET Core Using Outside-In TDD, by Pedro Moreira Santos

TDD and The Terminator – An Introduction to Test-Driven Development, by Layla Porter

Developing with .NET Core on AWS Using Rider, by Martin Beeby

Better Object Mapping in .NET with Dapper, by Kevin Griffin

HotChocolate: an Introduction to GraphQL for ASP.NET Core, by Michael Staib

We apologize to those of you who were looking forward to the Xamarin and Azure talks – unfortunately, those speakers were unable to attend on the day. We hope to hold their sessions later as separate webinars, so keep an eye out for updates in our blog.

If you have any thoughts to share, please leave them in the comments below. Your feedback will help us make next year’s JetBrains .NET Days Online even better.
