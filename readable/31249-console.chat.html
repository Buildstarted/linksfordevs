Yes way! Open your browser's JavaScript console right now to see a demo.

Anybody can set their username to anything so impersonation is really easy. Use common sense when chatting! People claiming to be mods or admins are lying as I use automated moderation tools. Thanks!

<script src="https://console.chat/load.js"></script>
