Debubble

Reading time: 2-3 minutes
Posted here: 06 Jul 2020

https://debubble.me/

This is Debubble, a publishing tool that facilitates a debate between two Twitter users.

Debubble will let you start a debate. Other users can then challenge your opening arguments. If you accept a challenge, you and your opponent will be able to engage in a public but distraction-free conversation. Debubble will make sure you wait for your turn before you can deliver your arguments. It will also limit each response to 1500 characters (roughly one page) and the entire debate to 12 turns. Instead of cheering for their side like sports fans, users will be able to signal the value they got from your conversation by starring the whole debate.
