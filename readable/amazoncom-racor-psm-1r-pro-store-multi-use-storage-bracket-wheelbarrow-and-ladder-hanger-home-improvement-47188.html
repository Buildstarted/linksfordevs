Product Description

The Racor Wheelbarrow and Ladder Hanger can be essential in helping you maintain your home, creating a place to store wheelbarrows, ladders and other bulky items so they take up less space in your garage. Using a heavy-duty hook, the hanger firmly secures one wheelbarrow, ladder, lawn chair or other item to the wall and out of harm’s way. Constructed of powder-coated steel with a durable epoxy finish, the Wheelbarrow and Ladder Hanger is designed to provide long-time stability, corrosion resistance, and rust-free storage.
