Sophie's Dice by Sophie Houlden

Reading time: 2-3 minutes
Posted here: 23 Mar 2019

https://sophieh.itch.io/sophies-dice

Sophie's Dice is a dice roller application that lets you roll many kinds of virtual dice, and make custom dice suited for almost any game.

Features:

  • Select dice from your collection, or enter dice expressions using standard dice notation
  • Support for many roll behaviours such as dropping highest/lowest X dice, explosion, dice pool counting, and more
  • Save roll expressions you frequently use, to re-roll them easily
  • Includes dice used for most table-top RPGs*
  • Easily edit dice to make them your own
  • Includes many pre-made dice, over 160 rollable dice shapes, and lots of images/sound effects for all kinds of custom dice
  • Display your own images on dice faces, and your own sound effects for when they come up**
  • Dice are saved as .xml files you can share with other people who have Sophie's Dice**
  • Input stats from your character sheet to use in roll expressions

* d20 system (DnD), Fudge/Fate, Narrative system (Star Wars, Genesys), Storytelling (Vampire), Lancer. If your RPG needs some other kind of dice, you can make them pretty easily :)

* *On android, these features require you to copy files to/from a Custom Dice folder yourself.

Documentation for Sophie's Dice can be found here.
