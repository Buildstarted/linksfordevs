Description

Online security is extremely important in our rapidly growing technological world. Oftentimes passwords will inevitably be leaked online because some online websites that we signed up for was hacked. We often don't even know when account information are exposed and will continue to use the same password for other online services and websites.

Flow Leak can help you:

* Check if any email account info was exposed from previous data breaches

* Verify if any passwords have already been leaked

* Provide notifications for future breaches

Note that this app does not check your current account status. It will only check if your account shows up in online leaked databases.
