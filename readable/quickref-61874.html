Experimental search engine for developers. Searches a curated subset of the web: official docs and community-driven sources. No JS, cookies, tracking, external requests or data collecting.

Details and operators:

  • Literal by default. E.g. foo bar is treated as "foo" "bar"
  • ~foo turns the word from literal to fuzzy. E.g. ~foo bar is treated as foo "bar"

  • site: narrows search to specific url
  • intitle: specific word in title
  • * wildcard
  • Reference cards (example)
  • Indexed sites, cards and bangs are community-driven

Bangs:

  • !gh foo redirects to Github search with foo
  • !mdn foo redirects to Mozilla MDN search with foo
