MESA.org is where Manufacturing Meets IT

MESA (Manufacturing Enterprise Solutions Association) International is a global nonprofit providing education and networking about Smart Manufacturing and MES/MOM. MESA members include manufacturers, producers, industry leaders and solution providers who are focused on driving business results from manufacturing information.
