Metro Payment Pilot

Reading time: 1 minute
Posted here: 30 May 2019

http://paymentpilot.wmata.com/

Metro riders are needed to be a part of the Metro Payment Pilot – a test of Metro’s secure fare payment system of the future.

Testers will use transit cards, contactless bankcards or mobile wallets on their smartphones. You can be one of the first to take advantage of this new technology, and provide valuable feedback to ensure the success of its full rollout! 

If selected for the Metro Payment Pilot, you will:

  • Test state-of-the art technology before it’s released to the public
  • Use special, pilot-only fare lanes at select Metrorail stations
  • Earn bonus incentives for your participation

Apply now to see if you qualify as a tester.
