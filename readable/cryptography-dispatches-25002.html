Cryptography Dispatches • Buttondown

Reading time: 1 minute

https://buttondown.email/cryptography-dispatches

Cryptography Dispatches is Filippo Valsorda's lightly edited newsletter. It tries to bring longer form discussion of the cryptography engineering topics touched on @FiloSottile, but broader breath than those covered at blog.filippo.io.

The goal is not to talk about what's new, but what's interesting, with enough space to elaborate on how things work. Also, it's a way to write more, so expect less polish than blog.filippo.io.
