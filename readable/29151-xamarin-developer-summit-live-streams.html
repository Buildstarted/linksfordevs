Xamarin Developer Summit Live Streams!

James

July 9th, 2019

The Xamarin Developer Summit is right around the corner! The two-day conference starts this Thursday in Houston, Texas. It will be packed with TONS of amazing sessions and workshops for Xamarin developers. Don’t worry if you aren’t able to attend in person this year. Enjoy the conference remotely through our Xamarin Live broadcasts!

Watch Xamarin Developer Summit Online!

Each day, virtually attend the conference including the keynotes and sessions presented by Microsoft speakers. After the conference, watch these sessions on demand through the Xamarin Developers YouTube channel. Be sure to subscribe to get notified.

The day kicks off with “A Glimpse into the Future of Xamarin” keynote with David Ortinau, Maddy Leger, and myself at 9AM Central time.


Throughout the day you will see some stellar sessions including (all time Central time zone):

  • 10:05AM Speed Up Android Build Times & Shrink APK Sizes – Jonathan Peppers
  • 11:05am: What’s New for Xamarin Developers in Visual Studio – Maddy Leger
  • 1:05PM: Page Object Pattern and UITest Best Practices – Sweekriti Satpathy
  • 2:05PM: Streamline & Simplify Events with Reactive Extensions – Shane Neuville
  • 3:05PM: Crafting Real-Time Mobile Apps with SignalR – James Montemagno (that’s me!)
  • 4:05PM: Partly Cloudy – Tips & Tricks to using Azure to the Fullest – Matt Soucoup

To start day two, our very own Donovan Brown will be presenting a keynote entitled: “Mobile DevOps with Xamarin, App Center, and Azure DevOps” at 9AM Central.
