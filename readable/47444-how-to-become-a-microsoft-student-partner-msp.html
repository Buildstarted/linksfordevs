How to become a Microsoft Student Partner (MSP)

I have been a Microsoft Student Partner since 2018 and just got selected for the Gold milestone!

Haven received lots of messages from various people asking how they can become a Microsoft student partner so I decided to create this piece, grab a cup of coffee and let’s get started with the application.

Microsoft is looking for student advocates who are ready to build a community in their school focused on Azure and help other students to learn various Microsoft technologies to solve problems. This is inline with Microsoft’s mission

“To empower every person and organization on the planet to achieve more”.

To apply, Kindly note that:

  1. You have to be studying a STEM(Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics) related course e.g Computer science/Engineering, Systems Engineering, Geosciences, Mathematics Education etc.

2. You must have a passion for making a difference, building vibrant communities, and sharing the latest tech with your peers.

3. Experience in community engagement is a huge bonus

4. You don’t necessarily have to be an expert at Microsoft technologies, there’s a learning and mentoring process you will go through in the program

Applications are always open and reviewed quarterly, so when you apply, you’ll most likely be expecting a nice looking success mail in the next quarter

So it’s time to apply!

Visit Microsoft Student Partners Page to apply
