Social Media Guidelines and Disclosures

Our goal in social media is to deliver timely insight and perspective from our teams and senior leaders, and to participate in conversation around topics important to you.

Given the regulations governing our industry, below are some important guidelines and disclosures that govern our participation in social channels, and how we interact with you.

Content

Invesco is not responsible for the terms of use or privacy or security policies at this or other third-party sites that may be linked to by this page or other social media pages. You use any third-party sites and materials at your own risk.

Material has been provided for informational purposes only and should not be considered as investment advice or a recommendation of any security, strategy or investment product. All investments contain risk and may lose value. Mutual funds are subject to market risk and volatility. Shares may gain or lose value.

For regular product updates and commentary from our portfolio teams, visit the Insights and Commentary tab of invesco.com/us. For information on our Institutional Separate Accounts, please visit Invesco Institutional.

If you have transaction requests or questions about your Invesco account or other customer-service issues, please contact Invesco Client Services directly at 800 959 4246.

Invesco Advisers, Inc. is an investment adviser; it provides investment advisory services to individual and institutional clients and does not sell securities. Jemstep Inc. provides a digital solution for investment advisors. Invesco Distributors, Inc. is the U.S. distributor for Invesco Ltd.'s retail mutual funds, exchange-traded funds and institutional money market funds. Each entity is a wholly owned, indirect subsidiary of Invesco Ltd.

Comment Guidelines

Invesco does not endorse any comments other than those we post ourselves. Because of the nature of product-specific speech, and regulations governing recommendations or financial advice, we will remove any comment with specific mention of securities or mutual funds. Many investors may not know that dispensing financial advice is a regulated activity. General discussions on the market are appropriate, but specific mention of investment testimonials or strategies is not permitted.

If your comment includes personal information beyond your name, or any information regarding your portfolio, we cannot allow it on our social media platforms. These platforms are public spaces, and common sense should prevail.

Our goal is to host conversation that is relevant and valuable to you. To facilitate this, we ask that you keep your comments within the purposes of the posts made on our individual social platforms. Any comment that is clearly off-topic, makes unsupported claims, or promotes unrelated content or spam will be removed. It should go without saying, but please refrain from inappropriate or abusive language, or personal attacks.

While we welcome constructive criticism and encourage debate, we may also block, in our sole discretion, any commenter who continues to post material contrary to the guidelines set forth above.
