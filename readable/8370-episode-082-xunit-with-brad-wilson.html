Brad has been a professional software developer for more than 25 years, working as a consultant, developer, team lead, architect, and CTO. He is currently working at Microsoft on the App Center team, having previously worked on the ASP.NET and CodePlex teams. He has been involved in the agile development space since 2003, and along with Jim Newkirk (NUnit 2’s author) works on the unit testing framework xUnit.net. In his spare time, you will often find him gaming (board & video), playing music, or on the water in his kayak. You can find him (@bradwilson) and xUnit.net (@xunit) on Twitter.
