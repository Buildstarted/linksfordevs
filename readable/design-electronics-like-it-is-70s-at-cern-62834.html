Design electronics like it is 70's at CERN

Linac3 was built in 1994 and generates almost all ions used in the CERN accelerator complex since. Despite the construction date in mid 90's, some electronic modules controlling the radio-frequency fields in the accelerating cavities date back to 70's. Needless to say, it is pretty much all good-old analogue stuff.
