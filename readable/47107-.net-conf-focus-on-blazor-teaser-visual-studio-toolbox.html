.NET Conf: Focus on Blazor Teaser | Visual Studio Toolbox

In this episode, Robert is joined by Daniel Roth, who provides a look at the upcoming (Tuesday January 14) .NET Conf: Focus on Blazor. This is a free, one-day livestream event that features speakers from the community and .NET product teams that are working on building web apps with C# and Blazor. He also gives a nice overview of Blazor.
