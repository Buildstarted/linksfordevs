Why IVPN - What makes IVPN the best privacy protection service

Reading time: 10-12 minutes
Posted here: 24 Nov 2019

https://www.ivpn.net/why-ivpn?pk_medium=website

Not all privacy tools are created equal. Our service is run with expertise, integrity and transparency to provide real privacy protection. Our commitment to these values is what keeps us focused on our mission.

High standards for privacy

When designing our systems, policies and applications, the first and foremost criteria is protecting you from online surveillance. Our servers don’t store any personally identifiable information, and we don’t log any data associated with your account, a fact verified by a no-logging audit. See our straightforward privacy policy to see how serious we are about this.

Building a suite of privacy tools

While a regular VPN service simply encrypts your connection, we offer more. With our built-in AntiTracker tool, you can block snooping ads and unknown web trackers from hoovering up your sensitive data. Try the WireGuard protocol in our apps to achieve unparalleled connection speeds. We keep innovating towards better experience and privacy protection.

A transparent, dedicated team

IVPN's ownership and team members are visible so you know exactly who is responsible for protecting your privacy. We regularly publish privacy guides for educational purposes. We also support organizations fighting for the right to privacy, such as the EFF and Open Rights Group.

Principles before profit

Many of the companies claiming to protect your privacy violate it every day by sharing your data. We refuse to use third-party tools or deploy web trackers that identify and monitor you. We don’t have affiliates so as to avoid misleading promotions. We also reject behavioral and individually targeted advertising, as they are detrimental to privacy.

Who is behind IVPN?

IVPN is run by a team of information security experts and privacy activists. The founding team is a group of security professionals who met whilst studying information security at the University of London (Royal Holloway). We’ve been around since 2009, far longer than most VPN services - you can rely on our hard-earned reputation to protect your privacy and security.

Privacy is necessary for personal autonomy

We believe privacy is a necessary precondition for the development and preservation of the self. The power to selectively reveal oneself to the world is under attack from governments and corporations. We are fighting back.

Freedom is necessary for an open society

We believe you have the right to freedom of opinion and expression online without interference or surveillance. Freedom leads to enhanced expressions of creativity and original thought. We are determined to protect this right.

Surveillance can not be the answer

Advanced surveillance tools like device monitoring and facial recognition technologies are being deployed at a rapid pace. We refuse to accept that these tools are necessary for a safe society and call on governments to roll back their use.

Privacy requires strong security

We need secure tools enabled by strong encryption to protect ourselves and shut out anyone snooping on our activities. With the right set of protections we can once again control what we share with those we don’t know and can’t trust.
