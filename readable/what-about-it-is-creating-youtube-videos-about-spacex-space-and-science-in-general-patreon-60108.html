Welcome to the Patreon page of What about it!?

Thank you for coming here!


Who am I?

My name is Felix Schlang, and I am what you could call a self taught space enthusiast! Years and years of reading books and watching videos (not unlike the ones I make) have accumulated a big chunk of knowledge about all sorts of topics. So I am inviting you to dive all the way in with me. To explore Space and Rockets and to conquer the unknown! What about it!? is the essence of the space nerds needs for knowledge!

Why should you support What about it!?

By supporting What about it!? you help me to continue the show in an improved way! Every Episode takes between 20 and 50 hours of work. There are two Episodes each week, so funding is essential for the continuation of the whole project. I have time and ideas. You can help me with the third factor: funding!

What will I do with the money?

Funding is essential for many aspects of the channel! Funding enables me to do excursions and trips to research the stories you want to see! It enables me to step up the game with better equipment and go the whole nine yards of journalism for you and everyone else, watching What about it!?

There are many different aspects of creating a well researched and quality Youtube Channel about Space. The more funding there is, the more quality I am able to put into each and every episode.

Thank you for considering a donation to What about!? Every Patron counts towards the goal of improving the channel and thus the content you can expect!

THANK YOU SO MUCH!
