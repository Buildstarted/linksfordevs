MOTORS

magniX has developed a family of electric motors designed specifically for your commercial aviation application. Be it a single motor application, or as part of a multi-motor aircraft, the magniX motors offer the same level of industry leading redundancy and reliability second to none. The magni250 and magni500 both share a multi 3-phase architecture and turn at 1900RPM enabling a direct to propeller connection. They both integrate with standard propeller governors allowing for seamless adoption of current and future variable-pitch propellers.
