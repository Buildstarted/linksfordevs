The Virtual ML.NET Community Conference

Reading time: 2 minutes
Posted here: 27 May 2020

https://virtualml.net/

The Virtual ML.NET Community Conference is a 2-day community driven conference on all things ML.NET.
The conference will be streamed live on Twitch and YouTube.

It’s 100% free and open for everyone. The talks will be both technical and inspirational. We hope you’ll join us!

Join the community lounge Slack channel to connect other ML.NET enthusiasts!

The Virtual ML.NET Community Conference is brought to you by the ML.NET community, and is hosted by two community speakers Jon Wood and Alexander Slotte
