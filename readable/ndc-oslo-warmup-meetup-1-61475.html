NDC Oslo Warmup Meetup #1

Reading time: 1 minute
Posted here: 08 May 2020

NDC Oslo takes place on 8-12 June 2020 - and this year, NDC Oslo will be completely online event. All the same great workshops, sessions, speakers, parties and conversations you've come to expect from NDC Conferences, but in an online format.

Between now and June, we're running a series of warmup meetups for NDC Oslo. Join our host Dylan Beattie for an hour of tech chat, a sneak preview of NDC Oslo Online, and a chance to ask questions about the online format and how it's all going to work.

We're also inviting some of the great speakers who will be appearing at NDC Oslo to share what they're working on. This week, we've got coding architect and best-selling author David Whitney joining us to talk about how he built CoreBoy - a Nintendo Gameboy emulator in .NET Core and C# 8.
