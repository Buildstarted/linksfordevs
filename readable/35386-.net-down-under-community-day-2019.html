On Saturday October 19, 2019, the Sydney .Net community will be coming together for a day of learning and networking, hosted at the Microsoft Reactor. This day will be an opportunity for Sydney .Net developers to hear about the latest developments in the .Net ecosystem from members of the Microsoft .Net and Visual Studio engineering teams who will be visiting Sydney. Morning tea, lunch and afternoon tea will be provided, with a chance to socialise with everyone at the close of the day.
