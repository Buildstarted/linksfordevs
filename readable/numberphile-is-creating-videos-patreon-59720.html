Numberphile is creating Videos | Patreon

Reading time: 2 minutes
Posted here: 16 Mar 2020

https://www.patreon.com/numberphile

Numberphile is all about numbers and mathematics.

Here on Patreon we're asking for help making our films that little bit better - and offering some treats in return.

We'll have plenty of bonus features for patrons, including appearing on our Wall of Fame at https://www.numberphile.com/patrons/

There's also a chance to have your name appear in the video credits.

On our feed we post occasional secret videos and photos shared only with patrons.

You may receive gifts in the post (make sure to include an address) and of course there's a chance to get your hands on our (in)famous brown papers!

Funds raised here on Patreon will be used to create more animations, travel to meet mathematicians and pursue some of our more ambitious ideas (stay tuned).

In true Numberphile style, we've had fun setting the reward levels.

Regardless of whether you can contribute, our channel is free for all. And the best thing you can do for Numberphile is just keep watching (plus maybe tell your friends about us).

Numberphile's main sponsor remains the Mathematical Sciences Research Institute, where we regularly visit and film the world's top mathematicians. The project would not exist without them.
