HTTPS Is Easy!

No, really, it's dead simple. This 4-part series takes you through the basics of adding HTTPS for free with Cloudflare.

Part 1: Adding HTTPS

Let's start by getting HTTPS configured on the site and all non-secure requests redirecting to the secure scheme.

Part 2: Optimising HTTPS

Let's now configure HTTPS to be as secure as possible, surpassing "bank grade security" in just a few clicks.

Part 3: Fixing Insecure References

Insecure references in the HTML can take away browser indicators and put users at risk - let's fix them!

Part 4: Encrypting Everything

Secure all the traffic not just between the browser and Cloudflare, but all the way back to the server.
