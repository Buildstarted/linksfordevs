Why are some channels on Roku not working? | Official Roku Support

by Roku
Reading time: 1-2 minutes
Posted here: 30 May 2020

https://support.roku.com/article/360049417393

"***Alert - Some channels are currently not accessiblemore details

5/29/20

Due to a global technical certificate expiration, select streaming channels on the Roku platform that rely on this certificate chain may stop working after 3:48am PT on May 30, 2020. We are in contact with impacted content providers who use this certificate to ensure they deploy a fix to their channels as soon as possible.Meanwhile current generation streaming player and soundbar customers can install a manual software update from Roku now.To update your software:

  1. On your Roku player go to Settings
  2. Select System
  3. Select System Update
  4. Select Check Now

View this support page for more help to update your software manually. (FAQ)

We are continuing to work on a software patch for Roku TVs. We are sorry for any disruption this may cause. We know TV time is important especially while we are all at home. Thank you.

We will update this article as new information becomes available.  Thank you for your patience and understanding.
