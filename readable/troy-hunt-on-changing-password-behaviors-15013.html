Collective Intelligence Podcast, Troy Hunt on Changing Password Behaviors

By Mike Mimoso

Passwords, death, and taxes, none of them are ever going away. Even Troy Hunt has conceded as much.

Hunt‚Äôs Have I Been Pwned website recently turned 5 years old, and for much of that time it has been the definitive place for computer users to determine their exposure from data breaches. Have I Been Pwned is also a model for usability in security, enabling a free and clearly spelled out answer as to whether account information has been compromised, where, and how. Hunt hopes that it and its sister service Pwned Passwords continue to be the catalyst for improved behaviors online.
