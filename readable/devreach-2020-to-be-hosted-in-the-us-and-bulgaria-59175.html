DevReach 2020 to be Hosted in the US and Bulgaria

Reading time: 3-4 minutes
Posted here: 02 Mar 2020

https://www.telerik.com/blogs/cant-get-enough-devreach-got-you-covered-on-two-continents

By popular demand we are bringing DevReach, Europe’s preeminent developer conference, to a new continent!

In the immortal words of Depeche Mode we “Just Can’t Get Enough” of DevReach. If you need to take three minutes and thirty-eight seconds to have a listen, I wouldn’t blame you. It’s a great song – one that will likely be in your head for the rest of the day (you’re welcome).

And while Depeche Mode probably weren’t singing about DevReach, we certainly are.

DevReach has been Central and Eastern Europe’s premier developer conference since 2006. With world-renowned speakers covering the latest .NET and JavaScript technologies, attendees of the Sofia, Bulgaria event have continued to come back year after year. And each year they leave inspired, with new technical skills and knowledge necessary to build the apps of tomorrow. 

Introducing DevReach US

As we entered this new decade, we thought perhaps it was time to share the DevReach love with developers in North America as well. We had been running a great event in Boston called JSMobileConf that we wanted to expand beyond just the mobile and JavaScript worlds so we decided to bring the best of everything together – JSMobileConf and DevReach – to create DevReach US! 

DevReach US will be hosted in Boston June 16-17. Collocated with ProgressNEXT, DevReach attendees will be treated to 40+ technical sessions delivered by speakers like Tracy Lee, Jen Looper and Jeff Strauss, plus they will benefit from the epic ProgressNEXT keynotes, the larger expo hall, and a really cool attendee party. Early bird tickets are available now for just $399.

Get Excited for DevReach Europe Too!

The original DevReach is not going away. In fact, each year we find new ways to improve upon the last. As always, it will be hosted in Sofia, Bulgaria October 20-21 with pre-conference workshops available on the 18th & 19th and post-conference workshops on the 22nd. We will bring you inspirational keynotes and closings, awesome sessions, and a new spin on the attendee party. Registration will open in early March and speaker and workshop announcements will follow shortly after that.

No matter where you live, we hope you’ll be able to join us at DevReach US or DevReach Europe. We are super excited to be able to share the event with you twice in 2020 – cause, well, you just can’t get enough!
