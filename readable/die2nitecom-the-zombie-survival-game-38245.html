Die2Nite: The first free zombie survival game set in a hostile world inhabited by the living dead!

Help your community or live (and die) alone...

Game preview:

The crow and the gazette, "The News Corpse", will give you a daily update on the unfortunate goings-on in your town...

Got an irritating neighbour? Want to see them swinging from the gallows? Go to their house from the main town screen and report them. That's the other side of community life.

Running around amidst the zombies in the World Beyond just to find some rusty metal is a popular and rewarding activity in Die2Nite.

Make yourself at home, it's much easier when you've decorated your house and built a laboratory to concoct all manner of amusing narcotics.

You will die often in Die2Nite. Very often. However, your soul keeps track of all your incarnations and exploits in the World Beyond.

The bank is a place for sharing and trust. Also a godsend for a would be tea-leaf looking to profit from their gullible neighbours. Choose a side, citizen!
