Empowered By Imposter Syndrome

by JuneteenthConf
Watching time: 24 minutes
Posted here: 20 Jun 2020

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qheqnbhldI8


Imposter syndrome is something that the industry continues to represent as a negative. My take on this as a black woman in tech is we need to turn all of our disadvantages into growth opportunities. Adding structure to our learning path is the best way of removing the negative image of feeling like an imposter and allows us to flourish. --- Valerie Phoenix is a Founder & CEO at Tech By Choice and a Senior Software Engineer at Mastery Logistics. She holds a BS in Psychology & Art. Valerie is passionate about creating secure human-centered digital experiences. As a successful self-taught developer, Valerie started Tech By Choice to give back to the community that helped her succeed.
