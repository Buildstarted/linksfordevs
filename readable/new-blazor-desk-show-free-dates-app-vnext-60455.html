New Blazor Desk Show (free) Dates! — App vNext

by Carl Franklin
Reading time: 2 minutes
Posted here: 07 Apr 2020

Wasn’t it Billy Joel who wrote the song “Say Goodbye to JavaScript?” Oh, that was me!

Since the Blazor Road Show was cancelled due to WWC, we’ve moved it online! I’ve scheduled several free 2-hour online webinars where I share everything I know about Blazor including building and using generic components, state management, binding, authentication, authorization, and JavaScript interop. I’ll even show you how to develop a Blazor WASM and Blazor Server version of the same app simultaneously.

Even though Blazor WebAssembly is still in preview, the component model is set in stone. That means code you write today will run on the release version when Blazor WASM ships in November with .NET 5.

Now is the time, my friends! I’ve never been more productive developing for the web.

All of the dates are listed at https://blazordeskshow.com but here they are just in case you don’t feel like clicking right now.

All times are Eastern Standard time

  • Tuesday, April 7: 7AM, 1PM, 7PM

  • Thursday April 9: 7AM, 1PM, 7PM

  • Wednesday April 15: 7AM, 7PM

  • Thursday April 23: 7AM, 7PM

  • Wednesday April 29: 7AM, 7PM

Links for Twitch and YouTube are already on the website https://blazordeskshow.com

The Blazor Desk Show is sponsored by DevExpress, who is offering a free suite of Blazor Components that you can download and use right now in your Blazor WASM and Blazor Server projects.
