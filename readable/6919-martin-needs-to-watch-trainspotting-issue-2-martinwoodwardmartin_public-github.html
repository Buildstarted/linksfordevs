I've heard other services having issues with items such as Sing. It is usually the result of rampant child processes.

The prevailing recommendations are to avoid using kill to deal with these processes, as it can often lead to immediate lockdown of the parent process for extended periods of time. Instead, the suggestion is usually to push those processes onto a secondary output display.
