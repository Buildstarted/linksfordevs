This event is in UTC+00:00 - Dublin, Edinburgh, Lisbon, London time zone.

Closing time in your time zone is 16 Mar 2020 at 4:59 PM.

Thanks for submitting to DDD East Midlands! 

Only talk titles and descriptions will be shown to attendees when they vote for the talks they want to see.

  • Please try to leave out any identifiable information from your abstracts or talk titles.
  • Feel free to celebrate and announce your submissions on social media, but again we appreciate you not publishing the titles of the talks you've submitted.

Please read our Code of Conduct to make sure that your talk submission aligns - we will get in touch to ask you to change anything that does not abide by the code of conduct.

  • You are welcome to submit a talk with a co-presenter.
  • We discourage vendor or sales talks, though you may have a slide or two about yourself or company.
  • You are welcome to submit more than one presentation but if more than one makes it into final selection only your top talk will be chosen. 

We encourage speakers of all backgrounds and experience levels! 

All communications from us will be using the email you submit as part of your profile, including notification of selection.

Can't wait to see you at the event.
