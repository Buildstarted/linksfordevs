Bitsy Game Maker by Adam Le Doux

Reading time: 1 minute
Posted here: 27 Feb 2019

https://ledoux.itch.io/bitsy

Hi!

Bitsy is a little editor for little games or worlds. The goal is to make it easy to make games where you can walk around and talk to people and be somewhere. I hope you enjoy using it!

- Adam

Notes

  • Bitsy works best in the Chrome browser.
  • Instructions for getting started are in Bitsy's 'About' tab.
  • Have questions or comments? Post in the community here on itch, or find me on twitter.
  • Bitsy is also available on my website http://ledoux.io/bitsy/editor.html
