A fresh ring was placed on the rotating weld jig around 8pm.

Edit 1: One of the staged rings was moved inside the tent around 11:30pm. That ring is being spun and it looks like a possible stiffener is being attached on top of it.

Edit 3: Crane grabbed a different ring (likely the one from Edit 1) and placed it on the just-vacated jig. Musical chairs but, like, with giant steel rings!
