Milton

Reading time: 1 minute

https://www.miltonpaint.com/

Milton is a free and open-source infinite canvas paint program.

Features:

  • Draw with infinite detail on an infinite canvas. It feels raster-based but it works with vectors
  • Your work is saved every time you make a change. There is no need for a save button
  • Milton files are very small.
  • Unlimited undo and redo which persists after you close the app.
  • Layers
  • Blur
  • Lines
  • Export your work to bitmaps of any size. Export to JPEG or PNG at any resolution your machine supports
  • It supports Windows 7 and up. It compiles on Linux and work is being done on a for-profit macOS version.
