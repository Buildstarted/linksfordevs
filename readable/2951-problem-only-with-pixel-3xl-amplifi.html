Problem only with Pixel 3XL : AmpliFi

I've been having issues with my new Pixel 3XL and my amplifi. Certain websites have issues loading, and upload is very slow. The Pixel works fine through other routers on the same network, and all other devices work fine on the Amplifi. Using a third party VPN bypasses the problem as well. Has anyone else experienced this? Two other people I know and I have the problem with this exact setup.

UPDATE: Further testing with the ISP found that it was just related to something with PPPoE. Thanks for everyone who commented
