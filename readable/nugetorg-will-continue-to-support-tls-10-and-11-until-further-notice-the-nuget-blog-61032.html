Last November, we shared our two-stage plan for deprecating TLS 1.0/1.1 on NuGet.org and actions you can take today to ensure your systems use TLS 1.2. In that post, we announced that NuGet.org would remove support for TLS 1.0/1.1 in April 2020. However, since then, our customers have faced a variety of challenges in the wake of the COVID-19 pandemic. In order to avoid any further disruptions to our customerâ€™s workflows, we have postponed the removal of TLS 1.0/1.1 support until further notice.

For enhanced security, we still recommend ensuring your systems use TLS 1.2. We will announce a revised date for permanent removal of TLS 1.0/1.1 support when the situation changes.
