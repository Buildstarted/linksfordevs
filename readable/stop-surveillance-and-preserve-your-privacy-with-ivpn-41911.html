No, true anonymity is not guaranteed using a VPN. Any VPN service promising anonymity is negligent or misleading its customers. Using IVPN with AntiTracker enabled is a significant step in preserving your privacy online ‚Äì further privacy challenges are addressed in our privacy guides.

What protocols do you use?

We use OpenVPN by default with AES-256 encryption with 4096-bit RSA keys. All our apps come with the revolutionary WireGuard protocol built in for testing, which enables significantly faster connection speeds and performance than other solutions.

I can host my own VPN. Why is this better?

Hosting your own VPN might be good for WiFi security, but it does not sufficiently protect your privacy. Your IP address is unique and can be tied to your personal identity, since you have to own or rent the server required. By using IVPN, your IP address is mixed in with a large pool of other users and so websites and other services only see the IP address of our servers.

How does AntiTracker work?

AntiTracker blocks requests on the DNS level from entities that try to track you across the web and in apps you use: adware, malware, social networks, data brokers and so on. We use a publicly available and constantly updated list to ensure accurate blocking.
