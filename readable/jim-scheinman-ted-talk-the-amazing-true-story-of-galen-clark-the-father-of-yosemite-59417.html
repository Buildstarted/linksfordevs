Jim Scheinman TED Talk: The Amazing true story of Galen Clark, the "Father of Yosemite"

by Jim Scheinman
Watching time: 4 minutes
Posted here: 08 Mar 2020

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jpccXl_B6Fs


In this short TED Talk, Jim discusses the true meaning of finding one's passion and leaving one's legacy by weaving in the incredible untold true story of one of the key 'founders' of Yosemite, Galen Clark, an early pioneer and failed entrepreneur, who contracted tuberculosis while mining for gold and was told he had only months to live, yet who lived to 96 and is a key reason that we have the beauty of Yosemite to cherish today. Enjoy the journey and celebration of this soft-spoken true American hero, Galen Clark, as we celebrate the 150th Anniversary of Yosemite as National Treasure!
