Description

Tech Fest will be held on the 10th and 11th of October 2019, returning once again to the Statehouse Convention Center ballrooms. Industry leaders in all things technology will journey from all corners to come together for these 2 days to form Arkansas's premier software development conference. We want you to be a part of the experience.

This year's conference will grant you access to 40+ top-notch sessions over two day’s time. We try to play as hard as we work. Your ticket also includes lunch both days of the conference, a pre-event party, an attendee party, an official conference t-shirt, and additional swag!
