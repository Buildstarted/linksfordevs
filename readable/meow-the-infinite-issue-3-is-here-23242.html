Meow the Infinite Issue 3 is here!

Reading time: 1-2 minutes
Posted here: 13 May 2019

https://mollyrocket.com/news_0051

That’s right, ladies and gentlemen  —  everybody’s favorite spacefaring feline is back in the brand-spanking-new Meow the Infinite Issue 3: The Prized Possession! That means for all of you delightful folks who already bought the Digital Deluxe Edition, you can download the high-res PDF from your download link right now. We’ve also updated the Art Book and the Wallpaper/Avatar Pack with brand new stuff from Issue 3, so don’t forget to grab those again, too!

If for some reason you didn’t buy the Digital Deluxe Edition, well, that just confounds all logic. I’m sure you’ll come around are realize you’ve made a huge mistake. But until then, we aren’t leaving you empty handed: the low-resolution version of Meow the Infinite Issue 2: Dead Man’s Ocean is now available to read for free on the web site!

And as always, if you’ve never read Meow the Infinite before, you can start from the beginning with Issue 1: The Golden Fawn.
