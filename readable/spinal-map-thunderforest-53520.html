Spinal Map - Thunderforest

Reading time: 1 minute
Posted here: 06 Feb 2020

https://www.thunderforest.com/maps/spinal-map/

Inspired by a classic film, this style goes all the way to 11! It demonstrates the power of custom map styles based on vector tiles, along with a tongue-in-cheek approach to cartography.

In addition to the metal umlauts, look out for re-intrepretations of common place names, while enjoying the overly dramatic theming!
