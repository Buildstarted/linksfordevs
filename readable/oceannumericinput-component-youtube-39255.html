OceanNumericInput Component

by Karl Shifflett
Watching time: 25 minutes

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HHPcjEeSiSc


This video describes the OceanNumericInput Component usage and why it's needed in Blazor line of business applications. The OceanNumericInput is a replacement for the stock NumericInput component. This component supports binding to Int16, Int16?, Int32, Int32?, Int64, Int64?, Single, Single?, Double, Double?, Decimal, and Decimal? data types. This component has a FormatString property, set as required. Displays culture-specific numeric separator, digits separator, and negative sign. For a great validation, experience try the Ocean validators. Ocean Validation Library Video Series: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=d0FkZ0l9JjA&list=PLFZfBw9eNzgxi0yd3sh28wv8HXRoVCodQ
