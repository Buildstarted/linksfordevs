Due to COVID-19 and proclamations from the Governor's Office, The May 2, 2020 Uniform Election has been moved to the November 3, 2020 General and Special Election.

The May 26, 2020 primary runoff election has been moved to July 14, 2020. The Special Election for Senate District 14 will also be held on July 14, 2020.

Based on the Trial Court's recent order, mail-in-ballots are a legal alternative to in-person voting for many voters while COVID-19 is in general circulation. Anyone interested should review the Trial Court's order and should continue monitoring the situation because the Trial Court's order has been appealed. Learn More
