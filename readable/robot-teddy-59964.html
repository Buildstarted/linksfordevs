What is Robot Teddy?

Robot Teddy is a collective of freelancers. The idea is that rather than all individually working for one large company, we make our collective expertise available to multiple developers. We all find our own work, negotiate our own rates, and work with our own clients - although we do take on projects as a company, with guaranteed access to all of our consultants.

For clients, we can pool our knowledge together, making us more than just a single freelancer. Introductions, market research, advice, and much more - Robot Teddy provides a home for some of the best business people to thrive in. Of course, we never break NDAs and do not share confidential knowledge.

We all live and breathe videogames, and our breadth of clients reflects that. From large investment funds, to platforms, to 2-person indie companies - we can, and do, work with them all.
