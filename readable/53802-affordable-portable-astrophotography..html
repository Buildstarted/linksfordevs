For a fraction of the cost of other star tracking mounts, The Nyx Tracker enables users to dramatically increase the amount of light captured, yielding spectacular widefield images of the cosmos. Designed with ease of use in mind, setup time with the Nyx Tracker's alignment laser is intuitive and quick. Whether you're a novice seeking an inexpensive entry into astrophotography, a veteran looking for a set-and-forget mount to supplement your kit, or a hiker looking to travel light, the Nyx Tracker will help you enjoy astrophotography.
