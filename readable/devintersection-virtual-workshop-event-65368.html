Keynote Speakers

The DEVintersection Virtual Workshop Event is excited to announce 2 bonus keynotes by Microsoft thought leaders’ Scott Guthrie and Charles Lamanna. Both keynotes will be hosted by George Howell, former CNN Correspondent and international journalist. Each morning will kick-off with one of the following keynotes to get your workshop day off to an exciting start.

  • September 1, 2020 / 9am PST/12:00pm EST – A Fireside Chat on trending topics in today's world, with Scott Guthrie, Executive VP of the Cloud & AI Group | Microsoft.
  • September 2, 2020 / 9am PST/12:00pm EST – Accelerating Developers with the Microsoft Power Platform and live Q&A with Charles Lamanna, Corporate VP, Low Code Application Platform | Microsoft

Both keynotes are presented free of charge for anyone who is registered for the DEVintersection Virtual Workshop Event.

Workshop Speakers

The DEVintersection Virtual Workshop Event

The DEVintersection Virtual Workshop Event will be presented LIVE over 2 days. Each day will offer a selection of targeted 4-hour workshops taught by the industry experts you have known from our in-person conferences. In addition, we will kick off each day with a pair of bonus keynotes by Scott Guthrie and Charles Lamanna.

A new decade has arrived, bringing with it a whole new way to work from home. It is a unique time in our industry. Business everywhere needs to focus in on the essentials of being productive and profitable in challenging times.

Workshops will focus on technologies like the Cloud, Artificial Intelligence, and modern application development. Adding these skills to your repertoire will help accelerate the projects your company needs. Take advantage of this unique opportunity to strengthen your skills and help your business not just survive but thrive.
