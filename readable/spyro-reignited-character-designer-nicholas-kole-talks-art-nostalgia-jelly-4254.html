Nicholas Kole is a character designer who’s worked on projects like the Dawngate Chronicles, The Wingfeather Saga, Shardbound, Disney Books, Super Lucky’s Tale and most recently, the Spyro Reignited Trilogy. He’s also developing his brilliantly imaginative world Jellybots on the side. I met up with Nick a few months ago and sat down to talk about everything from art to character design, personal work to studio work, fanfics to freelancing, and his advice for everyone from beginners to those hoping to get official studio work.
