Manifold Garden – Beta Sign Up

Reading time: 2 minutes
Posted here: 03 May 2019

https://www.alphabetagamer.com/manifold-garden-beta-sign-up-2/

Beta sign ups have reopened for Manifold Garden, solo game developer William Chyr’s epic physics-defying puzzle adventure set in a massive Escher-esque world filled with impossible architecture.

Previously featured on Alpha Beta Gamer back in 2017, Manifold Garden is now nearing the final stages of its 6 year development. It’s a first person puzzle adventure set inside an expansive world that’s filled with mind-blowing Escher-esque architecture. As you make your way through the massive megastructure you’ll solve physics-defying puzzles, discover secrets and cultivate a garden to open up new paths. If the scenery wasn’t disorientating enough, you’ll also be able to walk on any surface you see – so good luck keeping track of what’s up and what’s down!

It’s been a long time in development, but it’s obvious the amount of work that’s gone into crafting the mind-bending world of Manifold Garden. It’s the kind of game where anywhere you look you’ll be presented with a breathtaking view that’s worth of framing and hanging up on a wall. Sign up for the Beta to experience its impossible beauty first hand.
