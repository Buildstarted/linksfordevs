Hi, my name is Ari Hunt, I am 10 years old.This is my website about my thoughts on some of my favourite video games!

Call of Duty ghosts.(COD)

Call of duty is one of my favourite COD games because of one thing: The campaign. It is a really interesting storyline about a war with ghost vs federation. I like it because it has lots of action and you get to drive differant vechiles like planes and tanks. I would definetalty recomend it to other people Who like COD.

Call Of Duty black ops 4.

COD black ops 4 is my second favourite COD game.It has variaty of differant characters, each have differant abilities.it has lots of really cool maps and guns.

Forza Horizon

Forza horizon is a great racing racing game with cool cars and great areas to explore.But it gets a bit boring after you have compeated in all the races.

Fortnite

Fortnite is a battle royal game where you have to be the last person standing. It is fun and all, but you die so quickly to other players that are a lot better than you. Other than that, it is a really fun game.

Overcooked

Overcooked is a two player cooking game where you have to work your way through differant levels that contain differant types of food. It is really fun to play with family and friends.

Apex Legends

Apex legends is a lot like fotnite, but it is more realistic.it is also a battle royal game. You can choose between each legend who each have their own special abilitys.
