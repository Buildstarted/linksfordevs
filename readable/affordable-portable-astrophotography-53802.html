Affordable, portable star tracking.

The Nyx Tracker is a motorized barn door star-tracking astrophotography mount designed for those who want accurate widefield tracking without breaking the bank. The most compact tracking mount on the market, it's also the least expensive.

Regular price $95.00

For a fraction of the cost of other star tracking mounts, The Nyx Tracker enables users to dramatically increase the amount of light captured, yielding spectacular widefield images of the cosmos. Designed with ease of use in mind, setup time with the Nyx Tracker's alignment laser is intuitive and quick. Whether you're a novice seeking an inexpensive entry into astrophotography, a veteran looking for a set-and-forget mount to supplement your kit, or a hiker looking to travel light, the Nyx Tracker will help you enjoy astrophotography.

How does the Nyx Tracker compare to other options?

The Nyx Tracker is smaller, lighter, and much less expensive than other popular trackers.

Why track the stars?

In order to capture the faint light of the Milky Way, astrophotographers take long exposures. However, the earth rotates, making the stars appear to move in the sky! Without a mount to track the stars, photos of the night sky come out blurred after more than a few seconds. These "star trails" can be beautiful in their own right, but if any kind of detail is desired, a tracking mount is necessary.
