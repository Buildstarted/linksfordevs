Q: What are the Session Levels?

    A:

  • Intermediate: Understand the basics, but now you want to go deeper? Intermediate sessions build on your existing knowledge and bring new depth. You’ll integrate your existing foundation of key principles with the new insights you develop. From code to context, you’ll gain new understanding.
  • Advanced: Knowing is half the battle; even with advanced proficiency and applied experience, you want to learn more about your chosen focus area. Advanced sessions go into specific implementation detail on a given technical topic and give you the tools to level up. Demo-heavy and code-focused, these sessions will be immediately actionable.
  • Expert: Expertise is fleeting, because we always have something to learn. If you live and breathe a topic, your focus can narrow too much. Expert-level sessions will integrate the latest insights with your existing expertise, weaving together what you know with what you’re learning. You’ll leave better able to teach and coach others about the latest developments in your focus area.
