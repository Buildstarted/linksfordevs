The Experience team is a full stack team responsible for building customer workflows for managing authentication and authorization. We work closely with product and design to build the best experiences for organizations of all sizes, from small businesses and open source communities, all the way up through the largest enterprises and global scale communities. Join us if you're excited to remove friction from user's lives and make GitHub a home for all developers.

Responsibilities:

  • Write robust, maintainable code
  • Work with product and design to iterate on customer needs
  • Review code and participate in group discussions

Qualifications:

  • 2+ years experience building web applications at scale using Ruby on Rails
  • Experience with relational databases
  • Knowledge of standard methodologies: monitoring, alerting, metrics
  • Ability to empathize with a wide range of engineers
  • Strong written communication skills

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Remote work experience
  • Experience with SAML, SCIM, 2FA, and other IAM technologies
  • Experience with business workflows and requirements

At GitHub, we constantly strive to create an environment that allows our employees (Hubbers) to do the best work of their lives.
