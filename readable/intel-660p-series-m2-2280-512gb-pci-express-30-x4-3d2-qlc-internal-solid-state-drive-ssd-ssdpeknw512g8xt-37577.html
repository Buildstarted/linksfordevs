If this is put into a PCIe 3.0 x2 slot will it dimish the performance? I want something that is low price anyway since it is in a slower m.2 slot but don't know if this having less lanes will slow the performance of the drive.

A shopper on Apr 7, 2019

BEST ANSWER: Yes it will operate at half the speed.
While x2 is more than enough bandwidth for even a Samsung Evo it has more to do with the controller on the cards and how they divide up data across the available lanes. Data doesn't transfer along one lane until its saturated and than uses the next, it is done a little bit across all lanes at the same time.
While this will still be faster than any SSD, no x4 card will hit max transfer speeds in a x2 slot. Look for an older card with a PCIe 3.0 2x controller. Or get this knowing you can use it in a new board when you eventually upgrade.
