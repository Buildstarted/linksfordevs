Tickets - NDC Melbourne 2020

Reading time: 2 minutes
Posted here: 10 Jun 2020

NDC Melbourne 2020 is a 4-day online workshop event.

Ticket type Price
4-Day Pass (Online) $1450
3-Day Pass (Online) $1100
2-Day Pass (Online) $750
1-Day Pass (Online) $400

10% GST will be added to all ticket prices

Need an invoice?
Choose "Pay by invoice" under the Payment Method option. Please pay the Eventbrite invoice upon receipt. If you require a PO, please email info@ndcconferences.com.

Terms and conditions
NDC Conferences reserves the right to make changes to the agenda at any time due to unforeseen circumstances. Any changes to the program will be noted on our website and delegates should check regularly for updates.

Cancellation
You have the right to cancel your purchase of tickets within 14 days of placing your order. After 14 days the following terms apply: Cancellations a minimum of 60 days prior to the event: 50% of ticket price is refundable.
Cancellations a minimum of 30 days prior to the event: 25% of ticket price is refundable.
Cancellations within 30 days prior to the event are non-refundable. Force Majeure applies.
