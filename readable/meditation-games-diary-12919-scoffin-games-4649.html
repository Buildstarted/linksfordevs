Meditation Games Diary: 1/29/19

by Steven Coffin
Reading time: 1-2 minutes
Posted here: 27 Feb 2019

https://scoffingames.com/2019/01/31/meditation-games-diary-1-29-19/

I don’t want to ruin today’s game for people who haven’t played it. Mark your calendars for the 29th of January next year. Martin Cohen’s Meditations game involves petting a dog and it is a wonderfully cute experience that I recommend to everyone. Unless you dislike dogs, then the game isn’t for you. If you like dogs and have a good sense of humor, then this is totally for you and is the game of the year. No game will possibly beat “That one game from 1/29/19 where you pet a dog and it is funny” on my GOTY list this year. Unless a game is very good. Then maybe. Well, that is all. Is it because I am very tired and worked way too hard tonight? No. It is because I don’t want to spoil this amazing moment for you. Now, please excuse me as I go to sleep because I worked way too hard writing these tonight. Just remember to download Meditations and play it on 1/29/20. Write it down, get a tattoo of it, do everything you possibly can to remember that date.
