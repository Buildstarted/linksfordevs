Some people like their jobs

Reading time: 1 minute
Posted here: 17 Jul 2020

https://datadiaries.bearblog.dev/some-people/

Some people really do. Everyone has bad days but these people, this special breed, seem to optimistically push through to promises of career fulfillment.

Sure, I envy them. But I mostly try to avoid them. It's a bit annoying seeing someone else getting enjoyment and fulfillment from working a nine-to-five while the rest of us have to grind through.

If anything it should inspire me to look elsewhere.
