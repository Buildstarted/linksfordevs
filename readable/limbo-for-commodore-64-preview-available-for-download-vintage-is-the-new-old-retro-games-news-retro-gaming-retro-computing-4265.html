Limbo for Commodore 64 Preview available for download

by Vintage is The New Old
Reading time: 2 minutes
Posted here: 27 Feb 2019

https://www.vintageisthenewold.com/limbo-for-commodore-64-preview-available-for-download/

We all know that Limbo, the award-winning game released in 2010 by PlayDead, is being ported for the Commodore 64 by Søren Trautner Madsen for more than a year as we announced a while ago.

After almost one year of being in the dark with no further news other than the video preview posted in our previous news, the C64 version has again surfaced, but now as a game preview cracked by the group Excess on CSDB!

It is not clear if the game preview was released by Madsen himself or obtained elsewhere but after playing it to the end, I can tell it shows more or less what was in the video released in December of 2017.

UPDATE Nov/06: I managed to talk to Søren himself and he confirmed that it is a legit version. He was talking to the Excess crew and they helped him to put the demo together for the x2018 party. He says he had the idea to release the demo in a cartridge but that wasn’t possible, therefore the cracked version.

I’ve played Limbo on the XBOX and I can tell the C64 version plays exactly like the original. The grayscale palette works wonderfully and the animations are very detailed.

We are still trying to find out more about this release, and if it indicates the game is really in active development or this is the playable version developed by Søren a year ago. Meanwhile, you can try by yourself downloading it from the link below or watch my video playing it to the end (of the preview)
