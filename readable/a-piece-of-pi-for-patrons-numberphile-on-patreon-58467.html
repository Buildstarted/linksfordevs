A Piece of Pi for Patrons | Numberphile on Patreon

Reading time: 2 minutes
Posted here: 18 Feb 2020

https://www.patreon.com/posts/33529079

To celebrate reaching Pi Million subscribers, here's an exclusive treat.

All Numberphile Patreon supporters at the Pi Level ($3.14) and above will receive a unique Piece of Pi.

That’s a specially printed A5 brown paper with your own 2500 digits of Pi.

Each will be signed by Brady, who makes the videos.

To receive a Piece of Pi, make sure you do the following:

1. Be a Numberphile Patreon supporter at the Pi Level or above. We’ll be using the list of people successfully registered with Patreon at the end of February.

2. Make sure your postal address is included (and up to date) on your Numberphile Patreon profile… When you sign on, addresses are not compulsory… but if we don’t have an address, we can't send a Piece of Pi.

3. Don't send us your address in other ways. We will only be taking addresses from the Patreon profiles. Addresses sent on other ways such as DMs or email, etc, will not be used.

4. We can't take requests for specific digits/pages. However we will reach out to higher tier and longer-term supporters to offer them this opportunity later. 

More details and opportunities will be finalised next month.
