Bishal and Words

Reading time: 8-10 minutes
Posted here: 01 Mar 2020

https://bishalashim.wordpress.com/

We have been together for years

but you remained same to me

neither you changed nor I

I love the way you love me

the way you have hovered me ,

when I had everything and even

when I was empty handed

with full of evil fates on the lines of palms,

I had fallen down in the deep gorges of so called success

when the whole world was behind me,

when my forehead was full of the wrinkles of failure,

You were there for me

As a hope of ropes

when I was drowning in the oceans of fraustations

you were there as a source of inspirations

So I love the way you love me.

The smell of your presence had kept me alive

I would had breathe for last time otherwise

You are the one where my eyes can stand

and roll down the joys and sorrows over cheeks

The shoulders where I can stand my head

and stare at the horizon for hours in a deep silence

Your eyes are the most abstract ocean for me

where I can find the everything settled

my body, mind and the breathe I inhale

The one who had chosen a broken man

full of failures,stress,struggles and the man who never achieved

Still you are with me with a blossoms of motivation to lead

Never minding the world and their stuffs about me

The honey who had best tried to mixed herself with the most bitter me

Really I love the way you love me.

Bishal Ashim
