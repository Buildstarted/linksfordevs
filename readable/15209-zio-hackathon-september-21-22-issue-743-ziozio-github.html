ZIO hackathon September 21-22 · Issue #743 · zio/zio · GitHub

I'm planning a ZIO hackathon weekend on September 21-22 (possibly preceded by a day of informal talks), open to everyone—regardless of level, experience, or contributions to OSS, and of course, contributors to Cats Effect, Monix, or related ecosystems are welcome too!

Tentatively planning to hold this in Warsaw, Poland, as it's easy and cheap to get to from Europe, and most ZIO contributors are already located somewhere in Europe.

My current idea:

  • If you are a ZIO contributor, I'll pay for your lodging and try to find sponsors for food
  • If you aren't a ZIO contributor, then please come too—you can find lodging nearby or stay (at cost) wherever contributors stay

I think this could be a lot of fun and result in great networking opportunities, as well as brainstorming and useful contributions to the broader ecosystem.

What do folks think of this idea? And would you be able to make it???
