The state of the restaurant industry

Reading time: 2 minutes
Posted here: 17 Mar 2020

https://www.opentable.com/state-of-industry

As the COVID-19 pandemic keeps people home and some cities, states, and countries limit restaurant operations, our community of nearly 60,000 restaurants faces unprecedented challenges. We’ve summarized the data we have from the restaurants on our platform and are updating it daily.

Please support your local restaurants during this turbulent time if you can. OpenTable has put together several recommendations on how to do so safely.

To help our restaurant partners, we’ve pulled together a Preparedness Resource Center to offer guidance on handling downturns and temporary closures, and have waived our gift cards fees to restaurants. We’re also working with restaurant associations in their discussions for government relief.

We’re all in this together. We’ll continue to share data, offer insights, and do everything we can to support our partners until this crisis passes.

About the data

This data shows year-over-year seated diners at restaurants on the OpenTable network across all channels: online reservations, phone reservations, and walk-ins. For year-on-year comparisons by day, we compare to the same day of the week from the same week in the previous year. For example, we’d compare Tuesday of week 11 2020 to Tuesday of week 11 in 2019. Only states or cities with 50+ restaurants in the sample are included. All restaurants on the OpenTable network in either period are included.
