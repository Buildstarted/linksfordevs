Description

MongoDB is coming to SF! Join us for a full-day educational conference covering the fundamentals to advanced techniques of app design and performance management. You’ll hear from the team that builds the database and power users running MongoDB deployments.

At MongoDB.local SF, we'll also host our first annual Women in Tech luncheon. Luncheon attendees will be able to participate in additional networking opportunities and will receive special swag.

This day of learning will close with a happy hour. Register now to claim your seat.

WHAT YOU'LL LEARN

  • Insight into building applications with MongoDB Stitch, the serverless platform from MongoDB
  • How to deploy, operate, and scale your database on all major cloud platforms with MongoDB Atlas
  • What’s new in MongoDB 4.0, including multi-document ACID transactions, and how the latest version of MongoDB can help your business succeed
  • Best practices from the experts on topics including schema design, analytics, microservices, performance, and more
  • Register for hands-on, deep-dive technical sessions, equipping you with the tools you need to build and deploy your giant ideas
