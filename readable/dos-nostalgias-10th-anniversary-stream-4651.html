DOS Nostalgia's 10th Anniversary Stream

by DOS Nostalgia
Watching time: 168 minutes

Celebrating TEN years of DOS Nostalgia while drinking champagne, messing around with DOS games, and answering questions from the chat. First I try the new re-implementation of the classic DOS FPS Blood - NBlood http://nukeykt.retrohost.net/ Then I try and repeatedly fail to get the game D.O.G.: Fight For Your Life running with sound. The next game is Tempest 2000 Then it's Duke Caribbean: Life's a Beach Then it's Tetris Classic Then Commander Blood And then I get too drunk to play anything else.
