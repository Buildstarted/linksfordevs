We're sorry to say that TurboPower Software Company closed its retail business on January 7, 2003. We would like to express our sincere appreciation to our customers and the software development community that supported us so well for nearly 18 years.

TurboPower libraries available as open source
We're happy to say that we've released our programming libraries as open source, under the Mozilla 1.1 license. The open source projects are hosted on SourceForge, and can be accessed by the links below.

If you are an active user of any of these libraries, please consider supporting their open source projects so they can continue to thrive.
