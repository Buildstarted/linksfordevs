Are you tired of nagging your kids?  Sick of the clothes on the floor?  Had enough of their behaviour,  whining or fighting and not listening?  

Well, I was too.  So I developed a behavior and chore game-like system that has CHANGED OUR LIVES and other families’ lives for the better.  I've shared the system with neighbours, friends, co-workers and even strangers and every single family has praised it's benefits.  Every. single. family. 

It wasn't until I was unexpectedly let go from my 20+ year corporate job in retail that I realized just how much I believed in the system.  I was home with the kids playing the game and realized I wanted to share this idea with the world and pursue it as a real business.  Seeing how well it worked for us and those around me, I truly and honestly wanted to help more families.  So I spent over a year and all of my severance building it into a mobile app!  An app we believe will become the future of parenting in a digital age! 

THE APP -  allows you to HIRE your Kids for jobs around the house and FIRE them when they don't follow the family values & household or behavioural expectations.

Imagine your Kids WANTING to help around the house, WANTING to behave better and actually signing up for chores on their own without you having to constantly nag at them or repeat yourself a million times!

Our game motivates the Kids to take initiative vs. parents assiging or telling their Kids what to do.  We use Recognition, Reward & Discipline, teaching the Kids how to respect your family values, how to be a team player, be responsible and be accountable.  All while preparing them for the important skills they'll need when they get out in the real world.

The Kids love choosing from the HFK characters within the app and enjoy playing “employee”, earning their “pay day allowance“ and competing for the coveted Employee Of The Month reward!  And parents love seeing their Kids taking the initiative, learning to take responsibility, work together, having fun and feeling proud of their accomplishments.
