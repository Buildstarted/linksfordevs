Hack to the Future - NDC Sydney 2020 | Software Developers Conference

Reading time: 1 minute
Posted here: 11 Oct 2019

http://ow.ly/5yKb30pGVUF

Infosec is a continual game of one-upmanship; we build a defence and someone breaks it so we build another one then they break that and the cycle continues. Because of this, the security controls we have at our disposal are rapidly changing and the ones we used yesterday are very often useless today.

This talk focuses on what the threats look like *today*. What are we getting wrong, how do we fix it and how do we stay on top in an environment which will be different again tomorrow to what it is today. It's a real-world look at modern defences that everyone building online applications will want to see.
