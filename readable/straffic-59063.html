Dear Straffic user, we would like to bring to your attention that we have been reported that a security vulnerability has been found on one of the servers we use to provide our services. Following this report, we confirmed a weakness did exist and promptly patched it, in addition to fortifying our existing security protocols. As of now, all systems are secure and we did not find evidence of any data misuse or data loss. We continue to investigate and will notify if we find evidence to the contrary. Although we do our very best to protect the security of our service and deeply regret such a vulnerability has been found on our service, it is impossible to create a totally immune system, and these things can occur. We would like to express our gratitude for those of you who notified us, and ask that you help us keep our services safe.
Yours Truly,
Straffic Team
