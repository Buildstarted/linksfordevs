207 South Broadway

FEMMEBIT 2019 is a 3-day visual art festival in Los Angeles celebrating the work of over 75 diverse, enterprising and forward-thinking female-identifying artists currently based in Los Angeles. It takes place Friday, May 31st to Sunday, June 2nd 2019 at Civic Center Studios, located in the heart of Downtown’s Historic Core. 

The festival will include immersive installations, curated screenings, light and sound art, symposiums, virtual reality and augmented reality experiences and a variety of site-specific activations. Each day, FEMMEBIT 2019 will premiere programs showcasing artists whose work, in summation, enriches narratives explored through their individual practices. 

FEMMEBIT 2019 will also have a merchandise store selling limited edition artist and FEMMEBIT swag. Plus we offer a full bar tailored to be the perfect mixer elevating your engagement with the FEMMEBIT community. 

FEMMEBIT 2019 is a not-for-profit platform funded through donations like yours. We aim to keep all programs FREE and we need you to realize our vision. The suggested donation for all 3 days is $25, but there is no minimum or maximum. The deadline to reach our goal is May 31, 2019. YOU directly support over 75 artists by pledging to our campaign and YOU can make FEMMEBIT happen.

This weekend at The Other Art Fair, the women’s arts organization, FEMMEBIT, presents immersive virtual worlds championing the female voice across the art and tech continuum; exhibiting artworks that question perception through the vivid mediums of virtual reality, metallic paintings, and vibrant digital prints.  Featuring artworks of Tara Dilloughery, Amanda Stojanov, Kate Parsons, and Janna Avner.

Upon entering booth 116, you’ll experience the virtual worlds of painterly landscapes and intimidating industrial thoroughfares, invoking altered states and sensations. Through immersive computer software, oil paintings, and limited edition digital prints, you’ll be able to capture the sacred qualities of digital ephemera in various different mediums.

FEMMEBIT is featured at the Other Art Fair, March 28 – 31.

FEMMEBIT presents a pop-up show and shop for the Yoko Ono tribute pre-concert event as IMAGINE GRAPEFRUITS. Attendees will discover art through their mobile phones close physical proximity to selected locations both inside and outside Walt Disney Concert Hall. Artworks will miraculously appear to guide an immersive exploration of art ensconced in dynamic architecture. Join us for the inaugural SPRING/BREAK Art Show in LA! The show runs February 15 - 17 at The Stalls at Skylight ROW in DTLA, transforming a series of 32 former produce storage units. Hailing from NYC, SPRING/BREAK makes their west coast debut by showcasing the vibrant cultural fabric embedded in our sprawling city. Concurrent and in partnership with FRIEZE Week, the LA edition features over 50 local independent curators, artists, and gallerists.
