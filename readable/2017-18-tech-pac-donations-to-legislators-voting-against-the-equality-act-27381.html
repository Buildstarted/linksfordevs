2017-18 Tech PAC donations to legislators voting against the Equality Act

Reading time: 1 minute

https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1uFEz7gD_d8Fidcf1K5byNID11Ji5BKFpOMDZc4ShS_A/edit?usp=sharing&usp=embed_facebook

This is a spreadsheet of donations from major tech company political action committees (PACs) made to politicians in the 2018 election who went on to vote against the Equality Act in May 2019. The maximum legal donation is $10,000. All figures are taken from the Federal Election Commission website. Apple and IBM are not included here because they do not have a PAC.
