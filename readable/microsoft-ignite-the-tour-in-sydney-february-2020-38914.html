Microsoft Ignite The Tour in Sydney, February 2020

Reading time: 6-8 minutes
Posted here: 17 Oct 2019

https://www.microsoft.com/en-au/ignite-the-tour/sydney

Sydney

International Convention Centre Sydney

February 13–14, 2020

The event might be over, but there's more to explore. Catch the most popular on-demand sessions, ask questions, and collaborate with experts in the Microsoft Tech Community.

Microsoft Ignite The Tour brings the very best of Microsoft Ignite to a city near you. The tour provides technical training led by Microsoft experts and your community. You’ll learn new ways to build solutions, migrate and manage infrastructure, and connect with local industry leaders and peers.

20+

deep-dive sessions and workshops

Learn

Find new ways to modernize workloads and migrate to the cloud, dive deep into the latest cloud technologies, explore innovative client development techniques, discover new ways to manage your infrastructure, and more.

Connect

Meet with experts who build and support the Microsoft technologies you’re using and evaluating. Join guided discussions with individuals who are facing similar challenges. Connect with technical communities on new trends and shared interests.

Explore

Discover where your business can go next, learn about the latest developments in productivity, security, AI, machine learning and development practices through breakout sessions, deep-dive workshops, expert connections, and more.

On-demand sessions

Explore content from the event at your pace to continue learning. Top breakout sessions are now available on demand.
