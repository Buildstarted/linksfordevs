Growth

From a small startup to a Fortune 500 company, it has been an amazing journey and a new lesson learned at each step. Join me, a program manager at Microsoft, as we take a walk down memory lane to learn about the ways you should (or should not) build your local and global developer communities. In this session, we will cover the three pillars of success to helping you organize events, connect with speakers, and create everlasting engagement. Additionally, in this new virtual era, discover how to bring your events online to the exciting world of live-stream.

- Jayme Singleton (Microsoft Corp.)
