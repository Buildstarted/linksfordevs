Are you interested in building and operating mission-critical infrastructure in the cloud and on the edge? Do you want to design products that push the boundaries of messaging and event-driven computing? We are looking for a Senior Program Manager to help shape product strategy and drive day-to-day execution across the Azure Messaging Services team within the Azure Compute organization. The software and services you deliver will empower developers and IT professionals across the globe to deliver messaging infrastructure that forms the backbone of enterprise architectures and modern server-less application platforms. 

If you are interested in a product management position that pushes the boundaries of messaging infrastructure across cloud and edge, join us in Azure Messaging! 

As a Sr. Program Manager, you will work across the Azure Messaging product family with a focus on Azure Event Hubs and Kafka. You will help drive Azure Event Hubs, and you will be responsible for interfacing with internal and external customers, driving the product road-map and feature backlog, and delivering on a set of business targets including revenue growth and margin improvements. To accomplish these goals, you will work closely with software developers and other PMs in Azure Messaging and across Microsoft.

Responsibilities

Responsibilities

  • Design end-to-end product experiences  
  • Prioritize product backlogs and coordinate a regular sprint cadence with developers 
  • Orchestrate software releases and roll-outs across Azure’s global data-center footprint 
  • Deliver real-time telemetry covering consumption, performance, cost of goods sold (COGS), quality of service (QoS), and more 
  • Help coordinate incident response on outages and service degradation 

Qualifications

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree or higher in Computer Science or related field, OR equivalent industry experience 
  • 5+ years of professional experience working as part of software engineering team as a Product Manager, Program Manager, or Developer/Software Engineer

Preferred Qualifications: 

  • Familiarity with messaging technologies like Kafka and AMQP
  • Passion for delivering mission-critical infrastructure in a fast-paced environment 
  • Strong collaboration experience as well as ability to find win/win solutions
  • Proven ability to drive strong business results
  • Experience delivering cloud-based products and services
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
