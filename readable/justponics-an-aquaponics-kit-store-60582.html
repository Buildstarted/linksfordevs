Complete aquaponics kit designed for desktops

Aquaponics is a system that circulates wastewater from fish to cultivate plants, whereby the plants draw nutrients from the fish waste and filter the water for the fish.  The bacteria in the system help break down the toxic ammonia in fish waste and turn it into nitrogen, one of the nutrients for healthy plant growth. 

GET A KIT OR BUILD YOUR OWN

Our aquaponics kits are perfect as an all-in-one desktop system. They require little maintenance and all the benefits of an aquaponics system – learning and sustainability. If you are adventurous, you can get our failsafe microsiphon and build your own desktop system.
