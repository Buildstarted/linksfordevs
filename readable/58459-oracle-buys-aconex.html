Oracle Buys Aconex

Reading time: 4-5 minutes

https://www.oracle.com/corporate/acquisitions/aconex/

On December 17, 2017, Oracle announced that it has entered into an agreement with Aconex, a leading cloud-based solution that manages team collaboration for construction projects. The transaction has closed.

The Aconex project collaboration solution digitally connects owners, builders and other teams, providing complete visibility and management of data, documents and costs across all stages of a construction project lifecycle. Aconex has been used in over $1 trillion in projects across 80,000 user organizations in over 70 countries.
