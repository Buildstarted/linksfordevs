The Azure Storage team is hiring! 

 The Azure Storage team enables engineers around the world to store their data in Azure. We are performance critical; allocations and milliseconds matter. If you’re the type of person that is willing to learn about optimization and architecture at scale, the Azure Storage team is what you are looking for. Azure Storage is a cornerstone infrastructure platform at Microsoft, used by developers around the world to store all types of data for internal and external products alike.

Responsibilities

We operate at hyperscale - billions of objects and exabytes of data in hundreds of clusters across the globe. We spend each sprint adding new features and perfecting our platform in order to continue accelerating our exponential growth. We are looking for software engineers who are passionate about performance and scale to help ensure our product is as efficient as possible as we strive to increase our footprint. 

 You are: 

  • A determined engineer, someone who is disciplined about performance and architecture and cares about optimizing high-scale systems.
  • Personable and positive: We want people with a high emotional intelligence. You understand people aren't perfect-you have the patience to work through difficult issues with external and partner teams.
  • Open minded: You are passionate and have an opinion, but aren’t opinionated.
  • Driven: Interested in owning cross team initiatives and improvements to improve Azure Storage.

Qualifications

Requirements: 

  • 5+ years of industry experience in software development with any common OOP language; C++ preferred.
  • You have familiarity with or are interested in learning about operations at our scale. 
  • Bachelor’s degree in computer science or related field, or 4 additional years of engineering experience.

Nice to have:

  • Performance background: You are comfortable focusing on the details of the system. 
    Experience with distributed systems and/or storage/file-systems.
  • MSFT: AzureStorage
