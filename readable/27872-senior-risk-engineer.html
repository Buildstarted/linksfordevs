Taking risks, making bets!
At Netflix we are challenging the traditional risk belief. We are making a strategic bet around the concepts of risk quantification. Our risk program is a continued business bet around how to help our employees and partners increase their confidence and ability to:
• and make informed bets.
To support our investment in this space we need to take smart risks, and you will enable Netflix to lean into those smart risks by building a program that promotes using risk quantification, models and impact analysis to increase our confidence around our bets. We are looking for thoughtful risk-oriented  professionals to enable our mission and support our inclusive culture. In this role, you will help establish and execute on a broad strategic vision for the risk program at Netflix. You will not only work within the team but also cross-functionally with various teams across the organization. 
  • You have the passion to communicate how using risk can help inform decision making
  • You can work in an environment with limited direction
  • You understand and advocate for risk quantification
  • You continue to distill the benefits of risk quantification and communicate it to others
  • Our company culture encourages independent decision-making by employees
  • You have a desire to use risk quantification within the security domain 
  • Of the opportunity to create a Risk-as-a-Service program
  • Can lead and promote risk discussions
  • Can lead and support quantified risk assessments
  • Can model risk 
  • Understand relevant data collection, data cleaning and data analysis.
Netflix’s unique culture allows the security team to approach and address security differently. Our security approach is shaped around the principles of “Freedom and Responsibility” and “Context not Control.” Employees have tremendous freedom in their work, along with the corresponding responsibility and the accountability to do the right thing for Netflix.
