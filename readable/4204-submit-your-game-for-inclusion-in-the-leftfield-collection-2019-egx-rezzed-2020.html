Submit your game for inclusion in the Leftfield Collection 2019 • EGX Rezzed 2020

Submissions are now being accepted for the Leftfield Collection at EGX Rezzed 2019.

The Leftfield Collection is a space within the event that allows indie developers to exhibit their latest games and game-like projects free of charge. Everything is eligible for inclusion from highly polished commercial work to rough prototypes in their earliest stages. Games with non-traditional controllers are welcome, in fact, they're encouraged, so if you're wondering if your project is right for the Leftfield Collection the answer is yes.

Although there is no charge to exhibit, bear in mind that there are only around 20 spaces available and the submissions number in the hundreds. To give your game the best chance of being selected please complete the following form and read the accompanying guidelines. There's a handy FAQ here but if you have a question that isn't covered please contact the Leftfield Collection’s curator, David Hayward. Submissions close on the 18th January.
