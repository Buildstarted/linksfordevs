CNN reporter arrested live on air while covering Minneapolis protests

by The Independent
Watching time: 5 minutes
Posted here: 29 May 2020

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TIClA57jWmQ


A CNN reporter and crew have been arrested live on air while covering the Minneapolis protests over the killing of George Floyd. Black correspondent Omar Jimenez had just shown a protester being arrested when about half a dozen white police officers surrounded him. Mr Jimenez told the Minnesota State Patrol officers: “We can move back to where you like”, before explaining that he and his crew were members of the press, adding: “We’re getting out of your way.” The journalist was handcuffed and led away alongside a producer and camera operator for CNN.
