Crèche facilities at Conference

For families with children aged between 2-10 years old, we are offering a child-minding service. The crèche will be open between 8.30am and 5.30pm in the Condell Room for parents to leave their child/children during the conference hours.

The crèche will be operated by Crechendo, a specialised crèche service provider. Crechendo will provide a secure and safe environment where children will be entertained with a variety of age appropriate arts/craft, equipment and toys whilst supervised by qualified and experienced childcare educators.

You must book and pre-register for the crèche prior to attending as spaces are limited. The creche is for children aged 2-10 years of age.

Please read the DDD Melbourne Childcare parent information sheet so that you are aware of all of the requirements.

For more details on Crechendo go to www.crechendo.com.au. Provision of childcare is subject to the Crechendo Crèche Guidelines and Procedures, please read these in full.
