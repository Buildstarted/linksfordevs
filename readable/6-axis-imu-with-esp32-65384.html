6- Axis IMU with ESP32

by Makerfabs
Reading time: 2 minutes
Posted here: 08 Aug 2020

https://hackaday.io/project/173866-6-axis-imu-with-esp32

Description

6- Axis IMU based on ESP32 for gaming/learning, and quadcopter. basically, i want to make a easy-to-create quadcopter with ESP WIFI, or BLE conection, a platform that i can freely add some other sensors/camera on it ... so i make this board in advance to test the IMU, based on MPU6050.

There MOSFET drivers for motors on the board, but i have not tested it yet with motors. by now, the IMU output seems good ..
