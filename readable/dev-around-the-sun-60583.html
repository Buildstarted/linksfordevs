Dev Around the Sun

Reading time: 3-4 minutes
Posted here: 10 Apr 2020

https://devaroundthesun.org/

Learn from incredible speakers in tech during a 24 hour, worldwide fundraiser for Direct Relief for those affected by COVID-19

May 12, 2020

Dev Around the Sun is a group of technologists, from all backgrounds, with one singular goal: To raise awareness and funds for the treatment and prevention of COVID-19 related illness through interactive collaboration and learning within the technology community.

We want to ensure that no matter who you are and what role technology plays in your life, you have the opportunity to take part in a very important cause during these times, while at the same time being part of a vast community of people with shared interests as yourself.

By collecting funds via sponsorship and donation, we will be able to give back to those affected by this crisis, with the hope that folks taking part in this event will be able contribute in any way they can.

Sign up to stay in the know

We’ll send you an email when the schedule has been finalized so you can plan on when to tune in. You’ll also get an invite to the the virtual party we’re hosting in your timezone!

By sponsoring Dev Around The Sun, your company or organization will help combat the COVID-19 crisis, but we understand that that alone may not be enough reason to garner your sponsorship. Your sponsorship can help save and improve the lives of all people affected by the disease in every community where you do business.

Strong ROI

In order to defend your investment, there is an expectation that you will get a positive return on that investment. Your investment will dollar for dollar be contributed to fighting the COVID-19 crisis, naming your organization as the source of the donation. 

Reach audiences

What better way to reach your audience than by having your organization listed as a sponsor to a grassroots, community-driven event created with the sole purpose to connect the technology community along with giving to a cause? Your organizations likeness will be listed as a sponsor on the event website as well as named in the event sponsor roll-call during the event.  

Inspire your employees

A majority of employees feel pride in knowing when their employer commits to important causes that help the communities they live in. When you sponsor Dev Around The Sun,  your employees will be appreciative of the fact they work at an organization that cares.  

If you would like to sponsor Dev Around the Sun, we would love to talk to you about your interest and what we can do to confirm your sponsorship. Contact us today to learn more about the process.
