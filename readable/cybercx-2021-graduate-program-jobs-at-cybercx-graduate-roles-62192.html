Graduate Rotation Program | Graduates - 2021

   CyberCX is Australia’s greatest force of cyber security experts. In 2021, we are bringing on our first cohort of CyberCX Graduates.  If you are looking to jump-start your career in cyber security, we welcome your application.

  About you:

  • Recent Graduate or planning to graduate in 2020.
  • Passionate about cyber security.
  • Curious. You love a challenge and finding ways to solve problems.
  • Creative self-starter. You readily take on projects and are always on the lookout for the best way to get things done.
  • Technical aptitude and interest regarded highly, though not required.
  • Experience in Capture the Flags, Hackathons or similar activities regarded highly, though not required.
  • Demonstrated ability to learn quickly and adapt rapidly to changing situations.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
  • Eligible to work in Australia without restrictions.

  
Where:
  
We are accepting 2021 Graduate Rotation Program applications for roles in Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, and Canberra.

   If you are interested in Graduate opportunities in other CyberCX cities (Adelaide, Auckland, Oxford, Perth, and Wellington) stay posted for news later this year about our Graduate Direct Entry program.

   Rotations:

   As a CyberCX Graduate, you will be exposed to a wide range of roles within cyber security and work with a range of talented teams across the CyberCX business.  Over the course of 2021, you will complete three rotations.  Most Graduates will spend time as part of our Managed Security Services team and GRC or Strategy & Consulting teams.  Graduates will have the option to apply for a specialist rotation to close our their CyberCX Graduate year.

   Our Practices are:

  • Cyber Capability, Education and Training.
  • Digital Forensics and Incident Response.
  • GRC (Governance, Risk and Compliance).
  • Identity and Access Management.
  • Managed Security Services.
  • Security Integration & Engineering.
  • Security Testing & Assurance.
  • Strategy and Consulting.
  

Do you already know your dream job in cyber security?  Have dreams of becoming the next great penetration tester or security analyst?  If you are not interested in Rotations, but want to start your career with CyberCX, do register your interest in our Graduate Direct Entry Program and we will contact you when the right opportunity becomes available.

   What’s in it for you?

  • Hands-on experience across multiple cyber security domains.
  • The opportunity to learn from a highly respected, talented team of cyber security professionals.
  • Ongoing training and development opportunities.
  • Coaching and mentoring in technical and professional skills.
  • Varied and interesting work – no two days will be the same.

  
How to apply:

  • Complete the online application by following the link below.
  • Upload your CV.
  • Provide a copy of your academic results upon request.

Contact information:

Questions? Concerns? Need more information before completing your application? Please direct all queries to grads@cybercx.com.au.  We aim to respond to queries within 2 business days.
