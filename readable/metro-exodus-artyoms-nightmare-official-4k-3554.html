Artyom is a man that has lived trough EVERY imaginable bodily horror. He has been tortured, shot at, went trough severe radiation poisoning and lived trough a concentration camp where he had to fight with his bare hands for his life, yet his nightmare is not being able to help somebody. I love Artyoms character, an every day man that became a hero because he had the willpower to fight and survive anything and everything that was put in his way.
