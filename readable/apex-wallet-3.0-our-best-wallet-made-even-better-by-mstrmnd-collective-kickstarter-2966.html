Story

Based off of our most sought-after wallet to date, The Apex Wallet, this wallet features larger/increased corner radiuses and soft rounded-over edges (rather than sharp bevels) making this wallet even more natural in your pocket.

In addition to these subtle design improvements, we've upgraded the colors from a type I to a type II military-grade hard-coat anodizing for an extremely durable, nearly scratch-proof, finish. Waterproof, sweat-proof, and life-proof!

Like the original Apex Wallet, The Apex Wallet 3.0 is precision machined from a solid block of aerospace-grade aluminum and features our "quick-swipe" thumb-slot. It is capable of holding up to seven cards, plus cash.
