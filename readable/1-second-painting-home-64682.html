1SecondPainting Explained

It isn’t magic – it’s science. 1SecondPainting is a deep neural network trained on thousands of abstract paintings, including a number of pieces by Pollock, Delaunay, and more. This deep understanding of abstract art styles allows it to generate dozens of novel paintings in under a second.

Step 1. data discovery & analysis

Over 14,000 unique abstract paintings were scraped and compiled into a sizeable corpus.

step 2. cleaning & Formatting

The paintings were cleaned, pruned, and formatted according to best practices.

step 3. Training

A StyleGAN2 network was used to train 1SecondPainting through transfer learning.

step 4. fine-tuning & Generation

After training, the network designs unique, creative abstract art in seconds.

I created 1SecondPainting as a proof-of-concept to assess the utility of modern Generative Adversarial Algorithms in art and design (inspired by Gwern’s TWDNE). Technically speaking, the generator implements a pretrained StyleGAN2 network (FFHQ-512) fine-tuned through transfer learning on a corpus of 14,305 abstract paintings. On a Tesla T4, it takes on average 0.173 seconds per novel generation. Not bad!
