Host Bio

Adam Conover is the creator and host of Adam Ruins Everything on TruTV, as well as a life-long gamer. His brand of investigative comedy has garnered rave reviews in The New York Times and Paste Magazine, and has received hundreds of millions of views on YouTube. As a standup comedian, he performs at clubs, colleges, and theatres across the country. His proudest gaming achievement is that he once beat Nethack.
