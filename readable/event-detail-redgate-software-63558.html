Redgate Streamed is an events series, designed to bring together world-class speakers, educational content, and opportunities to network with peers.

In light of the postponement and cancellations of physical events Redgate would have attended, Redgate Streamed will aim to connect us, providing a platform for us to learn from each other and feel connected.

On July 2, we’re partnering with speakers based primarily in Australia and New Zealand. Confirmed speakers include:

  • Damian Brady, Senior Cloud Advocate at Microsoft
  • Troy Hunt, Microsoft Regional Director and Founder of Have I Been Pwned
  • Steve Jones, Microsoft Data Platform MVP & SQLServerCentral Editor
  • Greg Low, Microsoft Data Platform MVP and founder of SQL Down Under
  • Hamish Watson, Microsoft Data Platform MVP and founder of SQL Down Under

Full agenda available here.

For every registration that attends Redgate streamed, live or on-demand, we’ll donate A$1 to the Australian Red Cross and A$1 to World Health Organisation’s COVID-19 response fund, so even if you can’t join us live, be sure to register and you’ll receive the recordings to watch on-demand.

Join these industry experts for a day of knowledge sharing, networking and live demos at the latest in our series of virtual conferences.
