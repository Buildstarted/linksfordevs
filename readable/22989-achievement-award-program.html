Achievement Award Program

Emergency Operations Center Shelter Communications App

Alachua County, FL
Program Year: 2019

Abstract:
In a storm evacuation event, every second can make a difference. During Hurricane Irma, we found opportunities to facilitate communication and situational awareness so as to provide a better experience for those in our care and to enable Emergency Operations personnel to make the most informed decisions possible. We developed an application which allows staff to communicate and collaborate in real-time and to collect, aggregate and track the current population of shelters county-wide, so as to more effectively respond to the ever-changing needs of those in storm shelters. This application utilized cutting-edge technology to allow access to anyone the Emergency Operations administrators designate, without the need for a login, while restricting access to anyone else, and also to enable offline use while maintaining the availability and integrity of data at all times.
