Freshwater Place is just a short stroll from Flinders St Station and can be accessed via the pedestrian bridge from the Yarra River Promenade.

Several tram routes run along St Kilda Road, Queensbridge St, Sturt St and Flinders St with tram stops within a 5-minute walk.
