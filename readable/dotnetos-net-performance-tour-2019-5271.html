Frequently Asked Questions

When more details will be published?

There are here already, read the schedule above!

How will the meeting look?

Each member will present one presentation. Of course, there will be time for breaks. The whole will last about two/three hours. Then less official part comes to have conversations in more relaxed atmosphere!

What payment types do you accept?

None! This initiative is free of charge. Just come and have fun!

What will be presented there?

Detailed agenda is to come. But be prepared for low-level, yet still practical knowledge about JIT, Garbage Collection, memory usage, unsafe code, parallel processing, ....
