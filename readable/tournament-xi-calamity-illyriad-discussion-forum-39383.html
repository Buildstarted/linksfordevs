TOURNAMENT XI - CALAMITY


A brilliant blue meteor has struck the moon above the lands of Elgea, and sent the magical channels and ley lines of the world into chaos. Waves of wild energies are raining from the heavens, threatening to eradicate all stable arcane energies and decades of magic study.  

The Circle of Five, generally cloistered from the mundane undertakings of all others, has taken action in an attempt to prevent the annihilation of magic and possibly the whole of Elgea.  The Circle asks now that the armies of Elgea and the Broken lands amass at pylons they have specially constructed. These structures will draw energy from all surrounding it, shielding vital arcane junctions from being tainted with chaotic magic.  

Aside from the reward of saving all magic as it is known, the Circle has incentivised participation from alliances with bounties of magical reagents. 

This is an alliance-based, king-of-the-hill tournament that will run from the 26th of October to the 23rd of November.  

We wish you all the best of luck!

GM Cerberus

TOURNAMENT SQUARES - PYLONS OF POWER

The Tournament runs throughout the end of October to the end of November.  From one second into Saturday the 26th of October through to Midnight on Saturday the 23rd of November, any member of any alliance in occupation of these squares receives credit for holding the square.

Credit is based on the length of occupation of each square.  Time occupied by an alliance on each square counts towards the Alliance total. 

This credit is on an alliance basis - individual players cannot participate in this tournament.

You are not allowed to leave your alliance, or be kicked from your alliance while you have any armies present on one of the Tournament Squares.

All NAPs, Confeds etc are suspended for the duration of this tournament for any military interaction on the Tournament Squares.  The Peace of the Camp rule *will not work* on these Tournament Squares.
