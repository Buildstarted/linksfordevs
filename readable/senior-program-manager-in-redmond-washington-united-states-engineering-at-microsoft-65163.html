We're looking for an experienced product manager to deliver tools and experiences for Azure developers & devops customers that they will use and love.  One core aspect of the role is to drive the experiences for ARM [Azure Resource Manager] in Visual Studio and Visual Studio Code, enabling a primary method to define and deploy resources needed in Azure. 

You will also take the lead in defining the toolset we need to engage developers with our developer services including Azure Config Service, Azure Redis Cache, Azure Labs, and more. 

As a part of this you understand your customer's issues from top to bottom and you will drive tirelessly to ensure wide ranges of customers are successful building and deploying their applications using these services. 

You will be joining a past paced, collaborative team where your ideas become features that ship every few weeks.   

Responsibilities

Be a depth expert on our external and internal cloud customers through customer development, data, and strong relationships 

Define and drive metrics around engaged usage and customer success for our tooling products 

Advance customer successes leveraging our Azure developer services 

Design solutions rapidly through experimentation and direct customer validation 

Drive and plan multiple projects simultaneously, and communicate status to partners, stakeholders, and developer division management 

Research and analyze competitive cloud tools and platforms to distill the essence of industry trends 

Evangelize your features in the developer community through docs, samples, blogs, videos, and events 

Qualifications

Basic Qualifications:  

4+ years experience in Technical Program or Product Management 

BS degree in Computer Science or Engineering

Preferred Qualifications:  

Industry or consulting experience building and diagnosing distributed cloud applications 

Proven track record for defining a technical vision and leading it to demonstrable business results with the product 

Scenario and user experience design skills  

Hands on experience as a developer, program manager, consultant or architect for cloud based applications 

Ability to dig deep into technical challenges 

Analytical skills required to set future direction and product plans based on data 

Strong sense of teamwork, personal integrity, responsibility and accountability 

Excellent verbal and written communication skills 

Strong customer advocate, self-starter and problem solver
