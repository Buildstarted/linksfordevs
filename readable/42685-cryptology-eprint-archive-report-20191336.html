Critical Perspectives on Provable Security: Fifteen Years of "Another Look" PapersNeal Koblitz and Alfred MenezesAbstract: We give an overview of our critiques of proofs of security and a guide to our papers on the subject that have appeared over the past decade and a half. We also provide numerous additional examples and a few updates and errata.
