Become a Founding Supporter

Back the new paradigm for cloud storage.

Our goal is permanence.

Permanence means no subscriptions; a one-time payment for dedicated storage that preserves your most precious memories and an institution that will be there to protect the digital legacy of all people for all time.

How is that possible?

We are leveraging the same funding models used by museums, libraries and universities for centuries. As a public charity, we can pool all our one-time storage fees into a shared endowment. We use the interest on this tax-exempt investment fund to pay our ongoing operations and storage costs, in perpetuity.

We need a critical mass.

Our endowment will reach a critical mass when we have enough users, with enough endowed storage, to cover the cost of our operations. Each new user is a founding supporter that brings us one step closer to our goal.

So how does this work?

When you make a pledge to back us, we won't charge you anything to claim your storage and start preserving your memories today. And if we don't reach our fundraising goals then we won't charge you ever. There's no catch.

It's a win-win for our digital future.

We'll only charge you when we achieve our goals. In this way, we're all in it together. You can be a visionary founding supporter without any risk and we can build the momentum we need to achieve a self-sustaining future.
