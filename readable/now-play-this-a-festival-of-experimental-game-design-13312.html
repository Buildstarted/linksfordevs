https://nowplaythis.net/

"Now Play This is a showcase of surprising gaming delights"

Now Play This is a festival of experimental game design running at Somerset House in London from 3-5 April 2020, showcasing interactive and playful work as part of the London Games Festival.

In 2020 we'll be looking at games that relate to our theme of 'Breaking Point' - games are the aesthetic forms of systems, and we're looking at works that break or subvert them in interesting and creative ways. Expect everything from small scale remixes you can sit with, to new and exciting installations that get people playing together.
