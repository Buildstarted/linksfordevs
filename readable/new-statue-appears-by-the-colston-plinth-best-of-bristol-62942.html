New Statue Appears By The Colston Plinth | Best of Bristol

by Best of Bristol
Reading time: 1 minute
Posted here: 16 Jun 2020

https://www.bestofbristol.co/new-statue-appears-by-the-colston-plinth/

The battle over the Colston statue has continued today as a new statue has appeared beside the empty Colston plinth.

It features a bald man in a string vest on top of a bin, with a globe in one hand and a mobile in the other.

On his mobile phone it reads ‘England for the English’.

It is not yet known who has put the statue there.
