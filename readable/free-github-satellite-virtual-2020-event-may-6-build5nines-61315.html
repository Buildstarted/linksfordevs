GitHub’s virtual event GitHub Satellite Virtual 2020 is coming up on May 6, 2020! This is a FREE virtual event featuring developers working together on the world’s software, announcements from GitHub team, and inspiring performances by artists who code!

Add GitHub Satellite Virtual 2020 to your calendar!

You and everyone else in the world is invited to attend GitHub Satellite 2020 for FREE! In addition to tons of free sessions for you to attend, you can also register for paid workshops as well. The paid workshops cost $50 with 100% of the purchase going to organizations leading COVID-19 response efforts.

Go GitHub! Go community! Yay, Open Source!
