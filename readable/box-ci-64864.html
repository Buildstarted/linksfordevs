We provide the CI service

Builds run on your hardware

Only your hardware has access to your code and infrastructure

Best of both worlds

Box CI combines the advantages of third-party and self-hosted CI. You get full control in the one area you want it - the builds. We provide the CI service to manage everything.

Run build agents on your hardware

Our open source build agent runs on your machines and does all the work of coordinating and running builds. Run as many agents as you want, and spin them up and down as needed.

Your builds are just scripts

Builds are just shell commands running directly on your build machine. No docker, no complex yaml configuration. Your existing build script should just work out of the box.

Fully-featured CI service

Automatic build on push, build statuses in Github, full build history, cancel and re-run builds from the app, access control for your projects. Box CI does it all.

Our integrations provide the features you expect such as running builds automatically on push, and showing build status on commits and PRs.

We only request the permissions required to enable these features, and nothing more. Box CI never requests access to your code.
