Powerful Hardware, Sleek Software

The first Linux ultrabook with a Ryzen 4000 processor and KDE's full-featured Plasma desktop and hundreds of Open Source programs and utilities.

Starting at €899

A slender laptop that packs a punch

At the heart of the svelte KDE Slimbook beats the AMD Ryzen 7 4800 H processor, one of the most powerful CPUs for portable computers in the range. With its 8 cores and 16 threads, it can run your whole office from home and on the go, render 3D animations, compile your code and serve up the entertainment for your down time.

KDE: The Smooth way to Linux

Secure computing made easy

Surf the web; keep in touch with colleagues, friends and family; manage your files, enjoy music and videos; all that and more while using a system that you can trust and be productive with. KDE supplies the best environment and the widest range of programs for all your personal and professional computing needs, while protecting your privacy and giving you full control.

Use the inbuilt Discover app store anytime to add, remove and update programs you need. Enjoy thousands of the latest productivity and entertainment apps for work and play.

Surf the web with the Firefox web browser. Firefox is perfectly integrated with KDE's Plasma desktop, letting you control multimedia playback, volume, downloads and more from KDE's programs.

Explore your storage: add, remove and modify folders and files on your hard disk and or the cloud, preview images, documents, videos and audio files contents without having to open them.

LibreOffice is the productivity app that provides you with a complete office suite with word processing, spreadsheets, presentations, graphs, databases and more.

High Quality Screen

With its Full HD and 100% sRGB screen, the KDE Slimbook delivers detailed images and life-like colors making it ideal for editing photos, designing and painting, editing video or simply enjoying your favorite TV-shows and films as if you were at the movies.
