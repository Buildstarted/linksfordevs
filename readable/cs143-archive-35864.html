Summer 2012

This second iteration of CS143 built beautifully off of the first. In the interim since last time, I have taught three other courses, and many of my experiences doing so informed my presentation of the compilers topics.

  • Total students enrolled: 27
  • Lecture Topics:
    • Week 1: Intro to Compilers, Scanning, Introduction to Parsing
    • Week 2: Top-Down Parsing, Bottom-up Parsing
    • Week 3: Bottom-up Parsing, Earley Parsing
    • Week 4: Semantic Analysis: Scope Checking, Type Checking
    • Week 5: Runtime Environments
    • Week 6: Objects, IR Generation, IR Optimization
    • Week 7: Global Optimization, Register Allocation
    • Week 8: Garbage Collection, Code Optimization

Summer 2011

What a wonderful quarter! This was the first time I'd taught CS143, and was the first upper-level course I have had the pleasure of teaching. The quarter was jam-packed with cool concepts, and I was extremely impressed with some of the creations my students came up with.

  • Total students enrolled: 62
  • Lecture Topics:
