Consultant · Leader · Learner

Raph has 10 years of experience delivering software products. Currently, he's a consultant that's focussing on delivering projects using a combination of empirical and iterative approaches, regular client interaction, and a focus on outcomes and goals. He combines this outcomes driven approach with a high degree of adaptability, that's proven through his broad exposure to various organisations including: tier-1, tier-2, and government organisations.

Raph strives to ensure that the right problem is being solved, in the right way, and using the appropriate technologies. Raph has delivered many technical and non-technical projects including: data engineering, infrastructure automation, release pipeline creation, cloud migration, application stabalisation, problem-discovery, process improvement reviews, and workshops.
