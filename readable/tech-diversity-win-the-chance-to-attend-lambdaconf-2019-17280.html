At Signify we are proud to support tech diversity opportunities whenever we can, giving each person the chance to succeed. We want to help all underrepresented groups in tech so that everyone has equal opportunities available to them.

This is why we are running a competition for one lucky entrant to WIN a ticket to LambdaConf 2019!

The conference takes place from 5th - 9th June 2019 in Boulder, Colorado, United States.

LambdaConf is one of the leading Functional Programming conferences worldwide and there will be hundreds of passionate and aspiring developers there to expand on their knowledge, therefore, where could be a better place to learn!

The winner will receive one ticket to attend the conference only.

How can you win?

It's easy to be in with the chance of winning the ticket to LambdaConf.

All you need to do is email us at info@signifytechnology.com with your full name and contact details in order to be entered into the tech diversity prize draw. The competition closes on 6th May 2019.

We only have one ticket up for grabs so get entering!

The competition closes at 5pm on the 6th of May 2018. After this date and time, no further entries to the competition will be permitted. This competition is open to anyone from a tech diversity, underrepresented group, aged 18 years or over, except employees of Signify Technology and their close relatives and anyone otherwise connected with the judging of the competition. The prize is as stated and no cash alternative will be offered. The winner will be chosen at random and will be notified on the 7th of May 2019.
