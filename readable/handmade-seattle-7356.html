SEATTLE CENTER. NOVEMBER 16, 2019.

Handmade Seattle is an independent developer's conference exploring and understanding modern machines in detail. We take place at the Seattle Center Armory all day Saturday from 9AM PST.

The ability to peek behind the curtains means we can re-think debuggers, editors, terminals, and even drivers. We can contribute to the future of languages or create faster compilers. We can write web apps with fewer and simpler dependencies. We move modern software forward.

The conference is hosted by Abner Coimbre

Handmade has its roots in video games and hardware. Top-notch speakers from these industries will inspire us with original talks.
