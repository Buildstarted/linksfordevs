Dazzle your Fur Baby at Dog Daze at Lake Fairfax Park | Park Authority

The Water Mine is going to the dogs as it closes out the swimming season with “Dog Daze” on Saturday, September 7, 2019. Come to Lake Fairfax Park from 10 a.m. to 1 p.m. for hours of fun for the two- and four-legged members of the family.

Let your pooch take a plunge into the water park for some canine-only swimming. Stop by the Canine Resource Fair and visit with a host of dog-centric vendors.

The suggested donation is $10 per dog. Proceeds benefit the Fairfax County Animal Shelter and the Fairfax County Park Foundation. Dog food donations for the shelter will be collected, too.

View Event

Dog Daze is presented by the Fairfax County Park Authority in partnership with Hunter Mill District Supervisor Cathy Hudgins, and the Fairfax County Animal Shelter and Fairfax County Park Foundation, with support from Weber’s Pet Store.

View Facebook Event

Lake Fairfax Park is located at 1400 Lake Fairfax Drive, Reston, VA. For more information about the park or the event, call 703-471-5415 or visit Lake Fairfax Park.
