NYU Game Center Lecture Series Presents Daniel Benmergui Thursday, November 1, 2018 Daniel is an award-winning game designer and one of the organizers of the Experimental Gameplay Workshop, the one indisputably “can’t miss” session of the Game Developers Conference. In this talk, Daniel will dive into the design details of his recent release, the “pet puzzle crawler” Fidel. He will also give us a glimpse of his next game – Storyteller, a promising exploration of new ways to combine narrative and gameplay. To find out more about the NYU Game Center's events, classes, and more visit gamecenter.nyu.edu/follow
