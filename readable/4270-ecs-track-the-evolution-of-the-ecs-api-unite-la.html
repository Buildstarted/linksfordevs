ECS Track: The Evolution of the ECS API - Unite LA

Come take a look at the past, present, and future of the Entity Component System (ECS) API. Software engineer, Aria Bonczek from the Unity Data-Oriented Design team, will offer a few specific examples of why we made the choices we did with this API. And anyone curious about the ECS will get a good overview of the API and its standards.

Speaker: Aria Bonczek (Unity Technologies)
