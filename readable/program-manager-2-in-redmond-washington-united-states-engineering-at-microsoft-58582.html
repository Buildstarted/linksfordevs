Program Manager 2 in Redmond, Washington, United States | Engineering at Microsoft

Reading time: 5-6 minutes
Posted here: 20 Feb 2020

https://careers.microsoft.com/us/en/job/777599/Program-Manager-2

This is your chance to make a difference in the lives of millions of Python developers worldwide. We’re looking for a technical Program Manager who enjoys collaborating with other product managers, engineers, marketing, technical writers, and UX designers and researchers to design end-to-end experiences for Python in Azure. 

Microsoft’s Developer Division is chartered with empowering every developer and every development team on the planet to build great software faster. According to StackOverflow, 51% of developers use VS Code and 32% use Visual Studio. In the same survey, we learned that Python is growing 7.5% year-over-year, making it the fastest growing programming language worldwide. The opportunity for your work to be influential in the Python community is huge! 

Responsibilities

As a Program Manager for this role, you will improve the Python experiences in all Microsoft products. You will accomplish this through a variety of activities including:   

  • performing competitive analysis of other Python offerings;
  • exploring quantitative & qualitative data, and defining success metrics;
  • identifying customer segments, and forming problem & solution hypotheses;
  • designing surveys/questionnaires, and interviewing existing & potential customers;
  • crafting UX mockup workflows;
  • ensuring engineering teams are clear on what they are executing on and why;
  • writing specs, blog posts, and documentation;
  • building end-to-end Python demos of our Python tools;
  • work in the “open”, and engaging the OSS community;
  • working with marketing/PR teams and presenting at user groups & conferences. 

What should you expect? 

  • Teamwork: This position promises lots of cross-team collaboration. Expect to build lasting, positive relationships with teams inside and outside Microsoft.  
  • Technical Complexity: Expect to speak proficiently about deeply technical subjects and understand the nuances of customer workflows. Expect fun and inspiring technical work that grows your skillset as a technical PM. 
  • Agility: Expect new challenges to arrive every single day. Expect to collaborate, reason, and sort through priorities with your team.  
  • Customer-focus: Above all else, you should expect customer feedback to guide your work. To be successful you will show empathy for customers, be sensitive to their needs, and partner with others to deliver a phenomenal developer experience. 
  • Impact: The opportunity to have a lasting impact on the tools that Python developers around the world use.

Atleast 1+ years' industry experience coding with Python

Have 1+ years of experience as a professional developer, program/project/product manager

Everyone on our team of Program and Product Managers can and does code
