HAIM - Towards Controllable Text Generation

As a step towards controllable text generation, we developed HAIM, a language model that can fill-in synthetic text between a human-written beginning and a human-written ending. HAIM allows the user to guide the text generation more accurately than its predecessors, by feeding the model with an ending and a desired length, in addition to a beginning.
