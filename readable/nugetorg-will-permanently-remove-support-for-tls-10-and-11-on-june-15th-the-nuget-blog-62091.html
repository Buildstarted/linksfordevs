NuGet.org will permanently remove support for TLS 1.0 and 1.1 on June 15th | The NuGet Blog

May 25th, 2020

Last November, we shared our two-stage plan for deprecating TLS 1.0/1.1 on NuGet.org in which we stated that the permanent removal of TLS 1.0/1.1 support would occur in April 2020. However, in April, to avoid disrupting customers in the midst of the COVID-19 pandemic, we announced that we would continue to support TLS 1.0/1.1 until further notice.

Now that some time has passed, we feel it is important to resume our plans to help ensure our customerâ€™s security. As such, NuGet.org will permanently remove support for TLS 1.0 and 1.1 on June 15th, 2020. Prior to that date, please ensure that your systems use TLS 1.2.
