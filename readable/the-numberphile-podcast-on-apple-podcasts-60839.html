Highly Recommended

If Ramanujan counted each natural number as a close personal friend, then surely Brady can say the same for each mathematician he interviews. The ease and affability with which he directs the conversation, while knowing precisely when to listen instead of talk, make this podcast a delight to listen to. Every episode has something of worth, whether an amusing anecdote, a new piece of knowledge, or an intriguing book, article, or piece of mathematics to sink your teeth into.

A must-listen for anybody with any interest in maths.
