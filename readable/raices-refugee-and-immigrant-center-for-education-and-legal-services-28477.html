ABOUT RAICES

WE ENVISION A COMPASSIONATE SOCIETY WHERE ALL PEOPLE HAVE THE RIGHT TO MIGRATE AND HUMAN RIGHTS ARE GUARANTEED.

We are a nonprofit agency that promotes justice by providing free and low-cost legal services to underserved immigrant children, families, and refugees. With legal services, social programs, bond assistance, and an advocacy team focused on changing the narrative around immigration in this country, RAICES is operating on the national frontlines of the fight for immigration rights. We defend the rights of immigrants and refugees, empower individuals, families, and communities, and advocate for liberty and justice.

Our volunteer community has made it possible for us to assist thousands of undocumented families and individuals, refugees, and asylum-seekers.

Your donation provides legal services for our most vulnerable, bond payments to reunite separated families, and brings systemic change through our advocacy work.

America needs a new vision for immigration. Every new member of our society should be afforded rights and treated with dignity.
