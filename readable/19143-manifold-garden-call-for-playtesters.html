Manifold Garden - Call for Playtesters

Manifold Garden (http://manifold.garden/) is nearing the final stretch of development after 6 years.

I am looking for playtesters to play through the current build of the game and provide feedback on its design and narrative elements. Specifically, I am looking to refine the difficulty of the puzzles and to see if any of the levels are too confusing. We are also interested in bug testing and making sure the game is stable and runs well.

There will be constant updates to the build we fix bugs and finalize content.

Right now I am only looking for people using Windows.

The build will be distributed via itch.io (https://itch.io/app)
