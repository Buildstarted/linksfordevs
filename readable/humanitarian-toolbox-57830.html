What We Do

Creating Solutions

The Humanitarian Toolbox is a sustained effort to leverage technology and skilled volunteer communities to solve the needs of response organizations and communities affected by disasters.

Building Together

We strive to bring together the greatest diversity of contributors (across experience levels, opportunity, and disciplines) to grow their own skills while they design, code, test and deploy solutions for good.

Delivering Impact

Our "toolbox" of open solutions can then enable responders and organizations to deliver relief more efficiently and grow communities' access to information and resiliency as they prepare for, recover from and rebuild after disasters.
