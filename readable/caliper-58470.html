Measure what matters for hiring in AI. At scale.

Caliper enables you to evaluate practical AI and data science skills through automated bite-sized challenges on real-world problems.


Subscribe to receive product announcements.
Maximum 2 emails a month. Promise.


Create a challenge

Have some (toy) data for the machine learning problem you're trying to test for? We will work with you to have a recruiting challenge on that data up and running in minutes.


Invite candidates to participate

Once hosted, share invitation links to your challenge via email.


Evaluate and recruit

Get notified when candidates submit entries, see how they perform on the dashboard, and follow up with them to interview or recruit.

Practice machine learning challenges

Train models, make submissions, see how you do. This workflow is meant to be representative of a recruiting challenge.

Greenhouse

Automatically send Caliper challenges to assess candidates through Greenhouse. When candidates complete challenges, their scores will appear within Greenhouse as well.

Don't see the applicant tracking system you use? Please reach out to us at support@caliper.ai.
