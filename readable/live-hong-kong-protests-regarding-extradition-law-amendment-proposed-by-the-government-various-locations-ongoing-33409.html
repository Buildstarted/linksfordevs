Live map

103.hk - updates every 15 minutes during active protests

Legend translations:

  • Shading - density of people
    • Light: sparse
    • Medium: dense, arms length
    • Dark: very dense, shoulder to shoulder
  • Colouring
    • Orange: protestors
    • Blue: triads/provacateurs
    • Grey: police
  • Arrows - flow of people
    • > Slow
    • >> Normal walking pace
    • >>> Running / fast pace
