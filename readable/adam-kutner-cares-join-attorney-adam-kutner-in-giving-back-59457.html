Adam Kutner Cares | Join Attorney Adam Kutner in Giving Back

Reading time: 3-4 minutes
Posted here: 09 Mar 2020

https://adamkutnercares.com/

Join attorney Adam S. Kutner in giving back to these outstanding Las Vegas area charities. For nearly 25 years, Adam Kutner has not only fought to protect the rights of individuals in Las Vegas, but helped build the community by donating time and money to the many worthwhile nonprofits in the city. Join Mr. Kutner and lend a helping hand by getting involved with one of these deserving charitable organizations.

Make-A-Wish

Make-A-Wish Southern Nevada has been making a difference at the community level by granting wishes to critically sick children across Las Vegas to help them build the physical and emotional strength to fight their illnesses.

Learn More

Home 4 Spot

Animals have a special place in attorney Adam Kutner’s heart. A Home 4 Spot is a volunteer-based nonprofit organization providing foster care, medicine and permanent homes for abandoned dogs in need.

Learn More

Opportunity Village

Opportunity Village is dedicated to improving the lives of children and adults in the southern Nevada community with intellectual disabilities, bettering their lives and the lives of the families who love them.

Learn More

Threesquare

Three Square is Southern Nevada’s only food bank, built on a mission of providing food assistance to residents of Lincoln, Nye, Esmeralda & Clark Counties. Join Adam Kutner in giving back with Three Square.

Learn More

Catholic Charities

For more than 74 years, the mission of Catholic Charities of Southern Nevada has been to serve those in need regardless of race, religion or creed. Join Mr. Kutner and lend a helping hand through Catholic Charities.

Learn More

In a continued effort to provide assistance to the less fortunate of Las Vegas, attorney Adam Kutner provided over $500 in food and other goods to one of the area’s leading charities, Catholic Charities of Southern Nevada.
