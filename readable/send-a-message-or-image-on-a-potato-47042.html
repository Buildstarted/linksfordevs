Our Pledge to reduce waste

As we want our customers to spread laughter, kind words, and unexpected fun with our products, we do realize that we are using a valuable food resource to do so. 

Because of this, Potato Parcel donates one potato for every one sold. We have partnered with local food banks to help us distribute them to those in need. We're proud to say that tens of thousands have been donated  and consumed thus far! 

The rest of our potatoes go to you. Once your potato has reached the end of its life, we really encourage you to plant it to make more! Send us a video of you planting your Potato Parcel.
