A Deep Dive Into POWERful Serverless Applications

Far from being the latest 'tech trend', serverless applications help your organization scale quickly and efficiently. They take the hassle out of provisioning infrastructure, configuring web servers, debugging configuration, and managing security settings, allowing your team to focus on coding and testing. The one aspect where serverless functions fall short is when it comes to state management. When missing state management abilities, building up complex flows in your data processing will prove to be quite daunting, and often, will require you to provide custom mechanisms for enabling you to communicate between your functions. Join this session to learn how to build serverless applications that scale seamlessly and automatically and leverage the Power Platform to create reliable and low-code applications. 

Speaker: Simona Cotin

Frequently Asked Questions

When and where does the event take place?

POWERful Devs is being held at 08:00 PDT on July 15, 2020 until 17:30 PDT. We will be live streaming the sessions here on the website, on the Microsoft Developer YouTube Channel, and encouraging individuals to engage in live chat on Discord. Links and information will be provided in your email closer to the event launch.

Discord is a platform that makes it easy to communicate over text, video, and voice. This is where the attendees of POWERful Devs can connect with one another and discuss everything from the conference content to best places to order food delivery.

Will this information be recorded and can I access it later?

How can I stay involved with this community?

We are so glad you asked! The Discord chat will remain live as long as people are talking. It will not be managed by any of the speakers or Microsoft event organizers, however, this is your community and please continue to engage. Additionally, please continue to follow Channel 9/Microsoft Developer on YouTube where we will be hosting a weekly #LessCodeMorePower video podcast series for developers just like you!

What do I do if I need technical or non-technical assistance during the event?

Please email the alias powerfuldevsconf@microsoft.com to get technical help, report Code of Conduct issues, or receive non-technical assistance during the event.

What's the difference between the Power Platform and Power Apps?

Microsoft Power Platform is a set of tools that enables users to create custom solutions with familiar, intuitive technology to support faster data collection, surface real-time insights, and make informed, actionable decisions. Power Apps is the low code application development platform that spans Azure, Office365, and Dynamics365, as well as stand alone applications, empowering collaboration to solve business problems and innovate faster. 

How do I know if a session is appropriate for me?

The POWERful DEVs conference sessions are designed for all developer communities. Each session has been labeled with a learning level of 100, 200 or 300, to indicate the developer skill most appropriate for the content that will be presented. Please don't let a 200 or 300 level session intimidate you. The speakers will present material in a clear manner to enable various people with various coding experience to succeed.
