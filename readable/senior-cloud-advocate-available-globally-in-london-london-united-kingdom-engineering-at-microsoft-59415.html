Senior Cloud Advocate (Available Globally) in London, London, United Kingdom | Engineering at Microsoft

*** The location for this position can be across Europe ***

Join our team and make the world better for developers!

We are building the Developer Relations dream team in Azure engineering. Our global team is committed to making the world amazing for developers of all backgrounds. We’re excited to support and contribute to open source platforms, tools, and processes. We’re spreading awareness of Azure and enabling developers to do what they love; write, code, and learn. This is the heart and soul of reinventing Microsoft’s relationship with developers.

Sound exciting? This is your opportunity to join and be a key part of our mission! Learn more here: www.aka.ms/meettheteam

The mission of the Cloud Developer Advocacy team is to win the hearts and minds of Developers through authenticity, community and technical engagement. The Cloud Developer Advocacy team is first and foremost pivoted on enabling a healthy ecosystem for technical communities to create and build things in the cloud.

We are looking for Cloud Developer Advocates who will have a strong Big Data and Startup focus. As a Cloud Developer Advocate, you’ll be instrumental in building a large and vibrant community for Microsoft Azure. You’ll build and nurture great relationships with developers online and in your local city, create amazing samples and content. and will be focused on winning the hearts and minds of our technical audiences at scale. Also, our team is diverse and inclusive, our team embraces diversity and inclusivity in what we do.

Qualifications

Required Qualifications:

- Background in Software Engineering with the focus on Big Data, Distributed Systems, and Data Science and hands-on skills in Java and/or Python.

- Experience and skills in Apache Spark, Apache Kafka, and other open-source projects for large-scale streaming data, data ingestion and processing.

- Experience with large-scale data stores such as Apache Cassandra, MongoDB, or similar.

- Experience with of data warehousing and ETL workloads.

- Solid communication skills - ability to be both a strong technical writer as well as a dynamic public speaker.

- Passion for helping developers connect people and communities through the cloud.

- A self-starter that can manage and prioritize initiatives that will make our developers and platform the most successful.

- Experience working with 3rd party communities, social media and blogging.

Preferred Qualifications:

- Knowledge and experience of Azure, GCP or AWS cloud services for big data.

- Familiarity of Azure services for big data, including but not limited to Event Hubs, HDInsight, Azure Databricks, Azure Data Lake, Cosmos DB, etc.

- Experience with products like Confluent, Databricks, DataStax, ElasticSearch, Redis and other products relevant for big data.

- Understanding of fundamentals and internals of distributed systems.

- Experience in contributing to big data open-source projects.

- Experience in automating infrastructure for data workloads and setting up CI/CD for data pipelines.
