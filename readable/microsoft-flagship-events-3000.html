Microsoft Ignite

New Orleans | September 21–25, 2020

Microsoft Ignite is for IT pros, decision makers, implementors, architects, developers, and data professionals who want to expand their proficiencies. The event features Learning Paths providing unmatched opportunities to explore the latest tools, receive deep technical training, and get specific questions answered by Microsoft experts.

Microsoft Business Applications Summit

Dallas | May 6–7, 2020

The Microsoft Business Applications Summit brings together Microsoft customers and partners, including power users, business analysts, evangelists, implementers, and technical architects. The event provides a forum to learn how end-to-end Dynamics 365 and Power Platform can create solutions to drive business and land new cloud customers.

Microsoft Build

Seattle | May 19–21, 2020

Microsoft Build is where leading architects, developers, start-ups, and student developers meet to focus on the latest tech trends and innovate for the future through hands-on demos, interactive experiences, and assisted troubleshooting.

Microsoft Inspire

Las Vegas | July 19–23, 2020

Microsoft Inspire is where Microsoft partners meet to connect, collaborate, and celebrate as one community, while exploring new ways to accelerate success. Working with industry experts and Microsoft leaders, partners learn to overcome the obstacles confronting their clients today and anticipate the challenges they’ll face tomorrow.

Microsoft Ignite The Tour

Multiple dates and cities

Microsoft Ignite The Tour brings in-depth learning experiences to cities around the world, in a free, 2-day format that creates opportunities for IT pros and developers to learn new ways to build solutions, migrate and manage infrastructure and connect with local industry leaders and peers.

Microsoft Envision | The Tour

Multiple dates and cities

An invitation-only, single-day event held in multiple cities around the world, Microsoft Envision | The Tour is where business decision makers and senior-level executives gather to connect with peers, share experiences with real-world solutions, and explore strategies for building successful teams and empowering employees.
