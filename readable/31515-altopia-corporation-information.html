TLS/SSL security & privacy enhancements:

The rating for news.altopia.com at Qualys SSL Labs is an A.
For SSL, we now require TLS 1.0 or better (up to TLS 1.2). SSL 3.0 and SSL 2.0 are considered insecure and at Altopia we prefer real security over a false sense of security. As an existing customer, if you need a free month or two to offset the cost of a newsreader upgrade, please email support@altopia.com. Insecure port 666 is also offered as an option if you still need to use insecure SSL 3.0.

Referral Incentives:

Attention Customers: When a successfully billed new customer signs up and mentions your email address or the message-id of a Usenet post you make from Altopia (no spamming!, less than 30 days old), your account will receive a free month of service. Multiple free months are possible.

TLS/SSL For All:

We support TLS/SSL (up to TLS 1.2) for all accounts. If your newsreader supports NNTP over TLS/SSL, configure your reader to point to port 563 and you should be good to go.

IPv6:
