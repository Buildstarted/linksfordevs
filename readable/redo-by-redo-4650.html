REDO! by Redo!

Reading time: 2 minutes
Posted here: 27 Feb 2019

https://redo99.itch.io/redo

REDO! - Dark Sci-Fi Metroidvania inspired by the works of Tsutomu Nihei, Lovecraft and others. REDO! is a game about a girl trying to find another human in a dystopian world overrun by biomachines. It's a lonely and evocative game about exploring the unknown and overcoming obstacles to see what is left.

  • A dark and mysterious narrative
  • A gloomy, vast and interconnected city to be explored
  • Non-linear exploration
  • Slow-paced, methodical gameplay 
  • A diverse selection of weapons and tools
  • Ambient music with some experimental tracks

SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS

OS: Windows 7

  • Processor: 1.2 Ghz or superior
  • Memory: 2 GB RAM
  • Graphics: 256 MB
  • Storage: 200 MB available space
