NDC Workshops Q4 2020: Call for Speakers / Call for Papers (CfP) @ Sessionize.com

Reading time: 2 minutes
Posted here: 18 Aug 2020

https://sessionize.com/ndc-workshops-q4-2020

If you are a speaker with a workshop relevant to the software developer community, we want to hear from you! 

NDC Conferences is pleased to issue its first Call for Papers for NDC Workshops, its relaunched platform dedicated to independent workshops.

Online 

NDC Workshops offers speakers ultimate flexibility as all workshops will be online for the time being allowing speakers to conduct their workshop from the comfort and convenience of their home office. 

As the online forum is perhaps the most convenient and efficient way to attend a workshop, it makes it possible to appeal to a broader set of attendees who might otherwise not be able to attend an in-person workshop.  

Why NDC?

With over 20 years of experience offering in-person workshops and events, NDC is one of the most trusted brands in the software developer community. 

NDC Workshops will promote and market your workshop throughout the NDC community and beyond by leveraging its social media, and actively marketing towards key demographics.

NDC is synonymous with quality content and is a destination for learning and inspiration. We invite you to be a part of the NDC Community and submit your Call for Papers today!
