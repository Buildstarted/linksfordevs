As Chief of Staff, you will work closely with leadership team of IC3 in driving the rhythm of the business - thinking holistically and strategically about key operational rhythms, business processes, procedures and supporting the business and enabling effective execution. This includes the coordination of cross-team initiatives and an effective communications strategy within the team and across Microsoft.

Within this role, you will be focused on continuous operational and business improvement and effectiveness through means of using your expertise to identify issues that impede business group success. This will be accomplished in part through means of working proactively to address operational rhythms, business processes, procedures, aimed at improving the effectiveness of the leadership team, and the overall organization with every iteration of every process and rhythm of business.

Responsibilities

Business Operations & Rhythms

In this role, you are responsible for key operational rhythms and processes, creating a holistic view of the operational mechanics of the organization. This includes responsibility for:

  • Helping the IC3 leadership team to filter and balance priorities to align their time and focus with key priorities of the business including prioritizing and setting cadence of appearances for IC3 team events (e.g. all hands, leadership team meetings)
  • Translating dialogs/strategy into actions and/or projects that achieve leadership expected outcomes
  • In partnership with the HR Director, driving the internal executive communication function of the organization, including broad employee engagement and reinforcement of organization vision and culture through planning and execution of the leadersâ€™ communication strategy.
  • Building operational rhythms and calendars which anticipate the delivery needs of the organization and enable the leadership team to operate effectively and efficiently.
  • Creating a holistic view of the operational mechanics of the organization with appropriate pacing, linkages and information flows.
  • Developing and managing annual, semi-annual and quarterly processes specific to strategy refresh, technical priority development, etc.
  • Preparation for key product and customer events.
  • Driving key upward and horizontal review processes including regular business updates, Execution Scorecard meetings, including organizational communication and action definition, delegation, tracking and management.
  • Tracking status and managing communication on engineering and technical dependencies, particularly those which rely on delivery from organizations outside of IC3 including coordination of announcement efforts.
  • Leading key ongoing/regular operational review processes specific to customer growth, (service health, incident post-mortem, and proactive health metrics), and product deep dives.
  • Managing cadence, agenda and logistics for leadership meetings and offsite meetings.
  • Proactively identifying issues that impede business group success and providing solutions/recommendations.
  • This role is also expected to take point leadership on irregular and unanticipated operational topics that are operational in nature, including driving strategic initiatives.

Business Management

This role will manage processes that will support effective business and financial operations of the organization including:

  • Managing annual financial planning processes with the leadership team, ensuring coordination and integration across groups and proactive development of growth scenarios and business cases.
  • Tracking progress and actions against long range financial plans.
  • Proactive management of organizational budgets and spending.
  • Purchasing and financial approvals.
  • COGS, R&D, Opex, and NTE headcount management across leaders working closely with business managers and admins of each leader.
  • Acting as the interface to Finance and the CQ Vendor Management Leader.
  • Facilitate successful implementation of organizational plans.

Leadership

The role is a direct report to the CVP and is responsible for providing direct support to the IC3 leadership team. As a member of the Leadership Team, the individual is expected to be a full contributor to all leadership discussions and topics, participating in most relevant meetings and offering synthesized business perspectives relevant to influencing key strategic decisions.

Qualifications

  • 7+ years of related experience to business management and financials.
  • Demonstrated ability to influence executive leadership on complex business topics.
  • Systems thinker, able to manage a high degree of complexity and to distill information into simple messages, able to think both strategically at a global level as well as effectively developing key processes.
  • Exceptional cross-organization coordination and communication skills including ability to build comprehensive and compelling presentation and communication materials that span functions and groups.
  • Strong interpersonal skills including ability to build trust, influence, and foster relationships with diverse team members; ability to leverage relationships to drive consistency of approach and ensure delivery.
  • Ability to balance varying responsibilities that span rhythm of business, communications, executive management, and culture.
  • Passion to foster a culture of efficiency and high performance through initiatives, training & education, and communication.
  • Strong project management skills and ability to lead multiple high-profile projects of varying timelines simultaneously.
  • Ability to effectively collaborate, influence, and leverage outside resources to drive results.
  • Manages confidentiality without exception
