UPDATE: AT THIS TIME, THE NEED FOR DEMONSTRATION BAIL HAS BEEN MET, BUT THE FIGHT FOR JUSTICE CONTINUES.

Challenge racism, inequality and injustice.

Fight back against legal systems that target and harm low income communities of color.

Help protect the presumption of innocence in the criminal legal system.

Help secure the freedom of immigrants from ICE detention.

Your support for the Brooklyn Community Bail Fund makes all this possible and more!

We're honored by the recent outpouring of donations from individuals around the world and their commitment to pay bail for demonstrators demanding justice for George Floyd, Breonna Taylor, and all victims of our violent and racist police state. We're committed to liberating those arrested in current demonstrations against police violence and are working to coordinate our intervention with community members, grassroots organizations, other bail funds, and public defenders. Read our full statement here.

We continue to fight for and with the people and the communities who have been brutalized for decades by the criminal legal and immigration systems; both deeply intertwined and built to criminalize, oppress and incarcerate Black and brown communities.

THANK YOU FOR YOUR SUPPORT!
