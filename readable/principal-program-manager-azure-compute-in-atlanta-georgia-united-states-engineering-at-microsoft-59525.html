Azure Compute just began its expansion into Atlanta, where world-class talent is revolutionizing the future of the Azure cloud platform. The growing team is looking for a product leader to help carry Azure Compute’s Atlanta investments forward.  Are you ready? 

Azure Compute in Atlanta is where groundbreaking development and new ideas are making an impact for the new era of cloud customers worldwide.  Cutting-edge technology domains driven by our Atlanta teams span across Robotics, Virtual Machines, Serverless Functions, Kubernetes, Supportability, Portal, and Open Source.  As Azure Compute continues to increase the investments in Atlanta we are looking for a leader to carry forward the growing business, build lasting relationships with partner teams, and create a supportive culture that fosters innovation and teamwork.

Responsibilities

As the leader of the Azure Compute Atlanta Program Management team, you will collaborate across Microsoft to assure successful delivery of division-wide business priorities for the Azure Compute portfolio. You will guide product definition and service design for investments being built within the teams ensuring a deep connection with customers in partnership with the development teams. Finally, you will be looked to for further establishing team culture, operational models and personnel growth as our investments and team members grow in product stewardship and importance for the broader Azure business.

As the Atlanta site program management leader for Azure Compute, we look for proven experience leading distributed engineering organizations; managing engineering projects as well as coordinating efforts between different R&D organizations and geographical locations; and leading discipline excellence for Azure's Program Management presence in Atlanta partnering with leaders across functional lines. 

Qualifications

Basic Qualifications:

  • 7+ years of experience in a technical product leadership role
  • 2+ years of experience working with cloud technologies

Ideal candidates will exhibit: 

  • Personal drive and impact, a desire to roll up the sleeves and make things happen 
  • Collaborative attitude, ability to work toward customer and business goals while influencing across the organizations 
  • Passion for and track record for growing leaders and creating an inclusive environment 
  • Product thought leadership with technical curiosity and aptitude to quickly understand service capabilities, technical issues and customer needs 
  • Experience with cloud-based technologies across platform innovations and management. Ideally a portion of experience with Azure, AWS, GCP or other cloud platform providers 
  • Exceptional communication skills and interpersonal connection for the organization 
  • Understanding of Microsoft field and corporate organizations to help navigate and coordinate efforts across the company 
  • Ability to thrive in a maxtrixed environment 

What we are offering the ideal candidate: 

  • A fast-paced, exciting work environment where there are always new challenges 
  • Opportunities to showcase your leadership and technical skills to directly help customers  
  • Direct impact on product engineering decisions based upon customer experience and data 
  • Impact to the Azure business all up and the broader culture and environment within Atlanta 

This position requires approximately 20% travel to Microsoft’s headquarters in Redmond, Washington, as well as travel to meet with customers world-wide.
