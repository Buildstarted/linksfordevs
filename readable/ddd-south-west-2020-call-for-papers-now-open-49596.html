The DDD South West 2020 call for papers is now open! This year our event takes place on Saturday 25th April at Redcliffe Sixth Form Centre, Bristol.

We are looking for a varied agenda of topics and a diverse range of speaking experience and backgrounds, so please also share with friends / colleagues and encourage them to get involved.

If you are thinking about traveling by rail there is a special conference travel GWR discount available when traveling from select locations (e.g. a return from London would cost Â£44) - see here.

To submit your talks simply register for DDD South West 2020 and once logged in you'll have the opportunity to add a speaker profile allowing you to provide information about yourself. You can then proceed to add and manage and submit your proposed talks.

Call for papers closes on Saturday the 29th February.

Good luck!
