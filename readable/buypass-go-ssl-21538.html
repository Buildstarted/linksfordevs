How does free SSL compare to high-end SSL

It is free, it is automated, and it is Scandinavian!

A free SSL certificate such as Buypass Go SSL is reasonable for low-profiled website with little interest of hackers. It is also an excellent choice to secure communication between servers. Free SSL helps protect transmitted data by means of encryption. Users moreover, avoid non-secure messages in browsers. To lessen the burden of system administrators, Buypass Go SSL provides automated issuance and renewals of certificates.

For all corporate, commercial and public websites, Buypass recommends an Extended Validation SSL certificate. The extended validation process checks that an applicant represents a legal business entity with a physical address. An EV SSL certificate is in fact an identity for businesses on the Internet. The identity is visualised prominently in browsers. This makes it easy for your customers to be confident that your site is the real deal.

Getting started with Buypass Go SSL

Buypass Go SSL is the name of the SSL certificate you will obtain from Buypass CA using the Buypass ACME API. To obtain a certificate from Buypass CA, you need an ACME Client. We recommend you to use the EFF’s Certbot Client. It runs on most OS and servers. Certbot can be downloaded here. Certbot requires root-privileges in order to perform its operations. The Certbot will auto-install dependencies the first time performing an command using the “certbot-auto” binary. After Certbot has finished installing its dependencies you will be prompted for input.
