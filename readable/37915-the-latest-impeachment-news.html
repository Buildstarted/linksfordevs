Previously: An impeachment inquiry is underway! The inquiry stems from a whistleblower's complaint around a phone call Trump had with the president of Ukraine. The complaint was released publicly yesterday.

2019-09-27

  • The first subpoena in the impeachment inquiry has been issued! Democrats from three House committees subpoenad Secretary of State Mike Pompeo for documents relating to the President's call with the Ukranian President and any facilitation the State Department did for his personal lawyer, Rudy Giuliani. Will Pompeo comply? Well, "failure or refusal to comply with the subpoena shall constitute evidence of obstruction of the House’s impeachment inquiry." (Source: ABC News)
  • US Special Envoy for Ukraine Kurt Volker resigned his position. Volker had been named in the whistleblower report, which claimed he had helped organize meetings for Rudy Giuliani in Ukraine. (Source: The State Press)
  • Speaking of Rudy Giuliani, he has kept busy reading his text messages on cable news. Not only do they confirm (again) Guiuliani's involvement, they also implicate the State Department and Kurt Volker in particular. (Source: Vox)

What's coming next: The White House doesn't have a great track record with complying with subpoenas so that may be a thing. Deadline is October 4.
