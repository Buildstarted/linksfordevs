An Integrated Chronic Care Platform

Lark Health has transformed disease management and prevention, replacing episodic care with a combination therapy of smart connected devices, AI learning, and expert coaching. This digital care model helps payors and large employers lower costs and improve member and employee health.

  • Health data monitoring from smart, connected health devices.
  • Translation of health readings into insights to make healthy habits and behaviors last.
  • The only digital health company with outcomes in all of diabetes, hypertension, and prediabetes.
  • Scalable care with seamless deployment and enrollment that integrates and doesn’t silo.
