Vive la tarte

Reading time: 2 minutes
Posted here: 22 Sep 2019

https://vivelatarte.com/

Cast iron-baked sourdough

Our bread loaves are baked in small batches, about 3 dozens a day. We prefer traditional dutch ovens — rather than expansive deck ovens with auto-loaders — because it’s really fun to pull the lids off every morning and see how the transformation has gone.

Experience the Taco-Croissant aka TACRO

We're about pushing the boundaries in the bakery world — that’s why we got creative and came up with the TACRO®. It's a celebration of who we are and the many places we come from. Discover this unique creation at the Ferry Building and Silver Lake.

Brunch crafted by hand, every single day

From avocado toast to breakfast salads, we got you covered with our California-inspired menu. We favor local, in season ingredients that we work by hand, because it makes all the difference. Bread, custards, falafels, hot sauce, you name it, it’s all made right here. 

Coffee & Tea from the best purveyors

For our beverage program, we’ve partnered with like-minded friends: we source outstanding coffees from Justin & Jerad’s Sightglass Roastery and distinctive teas from Aaron & Gina’s August Uncommon. We also offer locally-made kombucha and fresh juices.
