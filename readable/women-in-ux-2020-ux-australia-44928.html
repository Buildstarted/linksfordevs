Women in UX 2020 - UX Australia

Reading time: 2 minutes
Posted here: 20 Dec 2019

http://www.uxaustralia.com.au/conferences/women-in-ux

Attention women UX practitioners and leaders!

Tickets to both workshops have sold out, join the waitlist to Melbourne or Sydney now. 

In 2020, UX Australia, along with Mags Hanley, will run a series of workshops to grow the number of women speakers, increasing diversity, and ensuring new voices and ideas are heard at UX Australia's conferences.

These hands-on workshops will be presented in both Melbourne and Sydney, the first being a half-day workshop in Melbourne on Tuesday, February 18. Our Sydney Workshop will be run on Thursday, February 20. 

The workshops will focus on:

  • Creating proposals

  • Writing presentations

  • Practising presentations

Together we will:

  • Generate ideas and topics, working out which ideas have the potential to become talks

  • Write two abstracts from the ideas

  • Create one proposal including peer review, iteration, and copy editing it so it is ready for submission
