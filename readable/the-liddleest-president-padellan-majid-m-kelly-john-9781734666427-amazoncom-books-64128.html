Biting satire from Majid Padellan and John Kelly. For most people, failure is an important part of the learning process, and an essential step in achieving success. In "The Liddle'est President", the protagonist has failed so spectacularly and so often that he eventually fails upwards into the White House. Having achieved failure at the highest level, and with nowhere left to fail, he inevitably crashes like a zeppelin designed and piloted by Icarus.

For the Resistance. Fascists and Nazis will not find much to like here.
