A new way to Try .NET

by by Cecil Phillip, Maria Naggaga
Reading time: 1 minute
Posted here: 21 May 2019

Learning a programming language is becoming a such fundamental aspect of education across the world. We're always looking for new and interesting ways to teach programming to learners at all levels.

Live from Microsoft Build 2019,  we had Maria Naggaga come on to show us the Try .NET project. She shows us how this simple tool will allow us to create interactive documentation, workshops, and other interesting learning experiences.
