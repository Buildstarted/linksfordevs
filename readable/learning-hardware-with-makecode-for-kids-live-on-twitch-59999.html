Learning Hardware With Makecode for Kids -- Live on Twitch!

Reading time: 2 minutes
Posted here: 24 Mar 2020

https://www.nnja.io/post/2020/learning-hardware-with-makecode-for-kids-live-on-twitch/

I’ve always wished that I could have learned about working with hardware and electronics from a younger age.

I hope to share what I’ve learned and hopefully inspire school-aged kids who are currently stuck at home.

When and Where

I’ll be live-streaming an interactive workshop about programming hardware on Tuesday, March 24th at 11am PDT.

Watch at:

twitch.tv/nnjaio

Participation Notes

I encourage all parents to participate with their children.

The content will be targeted for kids from 8 to 12, although hopefully kids of all ages will be able to learn something new.

Since we’ll be using Microsoft MakeCode all you’ll need to participate is a computer with a browser. The hardware will be emulated, so no device is necessary to follow along.

Not able to follow along live? Want to participate at your own pace? The live stream will be recorded and made available on YouTube later in the week.
