Do you have a passion for keeping people and data safe on the web? Feel energized by approaching problems from multiple angles -- wallowing in deep technical problems, empathizing with user needs and concerns, thinking through major business and strategic initiatives? Excited to work with a diverse and talented group of program managers and engineers who share your passion and focus?

The Microsoft Edge Security team is responsible for protecting our users and enterprise customers from attack and data loss. Our work includes securing our client and service code, designing and driving secure design processes and effective security response procedures, and designing and validating innovative consumer and enterprise security features to protect users and data from attacks, and ultimately keep users feeling safe on the web.

Keeping users and data safe on the web is our mantra and we set no boundaries in achieving that goal.

In this role, you will be a key part of Security Program Management team, working with a strong and motivated of engineers to define and execute on what it takes to achieve that vision, with particular focus on securing the client and service code, via fuzzing and secure design processes, and working with external and internal researchers.

We are in a period of rare opportunity. In January, we released the first stable version of the new Microsoft Edge team rebuilt around the Chromium open source project. The browser space is ripe for innovation and has renewed emphasis and support from Microsoft leadership. We have built a foundation that has been well received and now it’s time to seize that opportunity and build a better and more secure web.

Come join us and help build an amazing team and an amazing product.

Find out more about what the team is up to on our blog: https://blogs.windows.com/msedgedev/ or on twitter @msedgedev.

Responsibilities

As the a key member of the Edge Security PM team, your responsibilities will include:

  • Product Leadership Work with the Security leadership team to help define and communicate a clear product plan for how to ensure that Edge keeps our customers safe.
  • Community and Customer engagement Engage directly with the Security community and our customers to ensure that we have a great connection with the security community and have a deep understanding of our customers’ needs and environments, and ensure we have a tight and effective feedback loop with our engineering teams.
  • Product definition and execution Design and deliver innovative and effective features to keep our customers safe on the web.
  • Partner with Engineering Work closely with the Security Engineering teams to deliver on our product commitments, and be accountable for results.

Qualifications

The ideal candidate will demonstrate:

  • Planning and Execution: Ability to think creatively and strategically. Ability to plan, organize, and manage complex technical products, and/or community engagement programs.
  • Communication: Able to conduct insightful, multi-faceted conversations with business, product and/or technical decision makers inside and outside Microsoft. Conceptualize complex ideas clearly and convey those thoughts to others in a clear, effective and motivating manner.
  • Microsoft Values: As a member of the Edge Product Team, candidates must be excellent representatives of the Microsoft cultural attributes and leadership principles.
  • Judgement: Excellent product and technical judgment. Consistently demonstrating maturity and intellectual curiosity, helping the team pursue the best ideas.

Required Qualifications

  • 2+ years of related experience in program management / technical product management, building software products, from feature design and development to release.

Preferred Qualifications

  • Experience working directly with the Security community is a plus, but not a requirement.
