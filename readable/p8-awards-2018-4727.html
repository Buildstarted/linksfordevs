P8 Awards 2018

Reading time: 25-32 minutes
Posted here: 27 Feb 2019

https://www.lexaloffle.com/bbs/?pid=60323

The winners are announced in the cart and listed below.

Controls:

  • Left/Right to change award
  • (O) to reveal winners (hold to slowly cycle through carts)
  • Up/Down to change selected cart
  • (X) to load selected cart
  • Hide All in the pause menu to reset the winner reveals

These are an (unofficial) set of awards, similar to the Steam Awards, designed to celebrate PICO-8 carts.
The community nominated carts over the course of December, and voted on them over the course of January.
CLARIFICATION: For this set of awards, any existing cart could be nominated, no matter how old. As no PICO-8 awards events have been done before, it didn't seem fair to exclude any carts on the basis of age.

ALL OF THE WINNERS AND HONORABLE MENTIONS CAN BE PLAYED FROM THE AWARDS CART!

arggh okay I just submitted mine but this was too difficult to pick only 1 cart for each category when there are 4 years worth of carts to choose from!

What if (assuming this ends up being a recurring thing) we just vote on all years every time? That way it would be the same rules every time, and thus be more "fair", and we would have a chance to nominate our other choices in future years (and we'd just have to say a cart can't be nominated for a category it has already won). This would be similar to a "hall of fame" model like the one employed by the U.S. Library of Congress National Film Registry (where a film actually has to be at least 10 years old to be considered), versus an "Oscars" model where only the previous year is considered. I guess my point is that by opening it up to all years now (more of a "hall of fame" model), it would feel weird to then close it off and change it into an "Oscars" model next year. Both models are probably good for different reasons and should both exist, just not in the same awards system :p

The other possible issue I see is that when you don't know what other people have nominated, it's possible to "waste" your nomination if someone has already nominated the same cart...maybe nominations should be "open" in some way? I don't know anything about designing systems for nominations/voting...it seems hard to do; these are just some observations that went through my mind.

Also I think there should be a category for "cutest cart" or something like that :D because there are some carts that don't squarely fit into any of the existing categories but I think deserve recognition for how expertly cute they are :>

But anyway, designing/organizing an awards thing seems really hard and it'll probably never please everyone, so I do not envy you :D

For the future, I see the following possible improvements (if we want to move this forward to more official awards):

  • Have community voters (as now) and an "expert group" (around zep) that can also exert votes with a given weight
  • Ensure a higher number of voters (via different social channels) to make biases from small numbers less probable
  • Have a shortlisting mechanism for nominations to not have too many items for voting
  • Think about categories (maybe including more common things like genre, best developer, best graphics etc.) and avoid possible overlap between categories
