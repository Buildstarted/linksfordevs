Soft Data Limit

If you exceed your plan's data limits we won't just switch it off. We give you some leeway to give you time to delete those extra packages, upgrade your plan, or fix that process that is hammering the repository.

Package Distribution

Distribute packages across your sites using Agents and delta compression, cutting the time and network bandwidth required to aquire and restore those packages.
